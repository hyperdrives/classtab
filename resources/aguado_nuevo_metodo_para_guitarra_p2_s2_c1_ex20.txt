out the melody.

Estudio 15), and an exercise in "Escuela de Guitarra" (Parte Segunda,
Seccion Primera, Capitulo 6, Ejercicios 10). The first seven measures
in the earlier version are the same except for measure 2 (the 5th and
12th notes have the octave G, and the even numbered notes are Ds, not
Bs), and measure 4 (the 15th note has D in the bass, not E) -
