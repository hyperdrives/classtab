Notes
-----

sheet editions used - Boije - Mayence B Schott's Sohne, 11423
                      Edson Lopes (2011)
                      Thorlaksson, 1007

notes and expressions from the Boije edition.

"This long virtuoso piece, Allegro brillante, whose final chords resemble
those of many a romantic symphony, brings Op 60 to an end. Perhaps Op 60
was intended to be performed as a complete set, in which case the flourish
of the ending of this piece would serve to bring the performance to an end."
Tecla edition notes, 2006 - http://www.tecla.com/extras/0001/0345/notes.htm
