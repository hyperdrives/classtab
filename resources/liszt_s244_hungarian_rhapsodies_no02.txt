without the original score. I tried to be as precise as possible here, but
a text tab has its limits...

Duration Legend
---------------
H      - half note
Q      - quarter note
E      - 8th note
S      - 16th note
T      - 32nd note
X      - 64th note (and 128th, 256th notes etc)
.      - dotted note (note held for time and a half)
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes

Tablature Legend
----------------
g  - grace note
h  - hammer on
L  - tied note
p  - pull off
s  - slide
v  - brush down
NH - natural harmonic
AH - artificial harmonic
