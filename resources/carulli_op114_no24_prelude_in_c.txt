Legend
------

left hand fingering below tab      right hand fingering above tab
1 - index finger                   p - thumb
2 - middle finger                  i - index finger
3 - ring finger                    m - middle finger
4 - little finger                  a - ring finger

=    - note held

> >  - crescendo - getting louder
< <  - decrescendo - getting softer
mf   - mezzo forte - medium loud
mp   - mezzo piano - medium soft
sf   - sforzando - forced, strongly accented

CI____   - barre on the 1st fret (using the 1st finger)
CII___   - barre on the 2nd fret (using the 1st finger)
CIII__   - barre on the 3rd fret (using the 1st finger)
