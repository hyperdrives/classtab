MASCHERADA - Anonymous
======================

Tabbed by Rene Puchinger - rene.puchinger@seznam.cz
                           http://citadel.wz.cz

This is quite fast Renaissance piece. As easy as Canzone in D.
I'd recommend you to listen the MIDI file.
Let me know if you think there is any error.
