Sorry that the tab is kind of long, but I did not want to cram everything
together. I only used the repeat sign twice the whole song, otherwise it
is the entire song. Some parts repeat, but I just put the part again
since I am not good enough to put in measures. Tempo changes many times
throughout the song, as in most flamenco songs. This tab may not make a
whole lot of sense unless you have heard the song. I'm sure that this is
not completely correct either so I'd be glad to hear any suggestions you
may have. I also have done Down the Long Road, and large portions of
Nights in White Satin and King of the Mountain Hall off of At Home With
With Esteban. Email me and I'll send you what I have.

Tune up 1/2 step to F (F,Bb,Eb,Ab,C,F)
