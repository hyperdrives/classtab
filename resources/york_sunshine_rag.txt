To make it easy to learn this song, listen to a MIDI or a video.
It has a lot of tricky timings that can't be noticed in this tab.

x stands for dead notes. Play them with your right hand but mute
them with your left hand (You can rest your left hand over the strings)

M indicates to mute the bass note with your right hand (as in palm muting for
the electric guitar). This is a tricky thing to do in this song, if you want,
or if it's too hard, you can play it without doing it.
