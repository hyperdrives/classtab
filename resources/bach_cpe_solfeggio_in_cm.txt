Solfeggietto  C.P.E.Bach
This was originaly a keyboard piece, but seems to lend itself
very well to the guitar. Great for practicing taps, hammers,
sweeps etc. You even need a pinched harmonic unless your guitar
has 25 frets....
I haven't anotated the fingerings/techniques I use, as everyone
has their own preferences. However if you really get stuck, I
could be persuaded to provide this info for the odd bar or two.
