For those new to ascii tablature:

   12p8    pull-off
   2h3p2   trill (fast hammer-on and pull-off)
   !5      natural harmonic
   ~       vibrato

   above the tab i have indicated some help with fingering:

   p       i       m       a
 = pulgar, indice, medio,  annular
 = thumb,  index,  middle, ring

   these are the (spanish) conventions for the right hand fingering

   the 1 2 3 4 below the tab are recommended left hand fingerings
   for maximum speed (if you wanna play the piece as fast as morel!)
   1, 2, 3, 4 = index, middle, ring, little fingers

   C3, C8 etc indicate a barre at that fret

anyways, have fun...this is a fantastic piece.
if you like it, be sure to check out 'Misionera' by Jorge Morel.

some notes:
there are many different recordings of this song floating around.
this tab is a transcription of morel's own recording, from his
'virtuoso' album.
probably more widespread is jason vieaux recording on 'cavatina'
this has a few little nice changes, at the start of the piece he
plays the first four bars twice and there are some measures which
sound a bit different, i.e. like:

 C3......
|----3---------0---|
|----5---5---------|
|----4---4---------|
|--------5---------|
|-3----------------|
|----------0-------|

and change bars 49 through 52 to this:

                    C2...........         harmonics............
|-0---------------|-----3---2h3p2----|-0-------------!12--|--------------||
|-----0-----------|-----4------------|----!12-----!12-----|--------------||
|-------0---------|-----2------------|-------!12----------|--------------||
|-----------2-----|------------------|--------------------|--------------||
|-------------2---|-----2------------|--------------------|--------------||
|-0---------------|-0----------------|-0------------------|-!12-----0----||


but most notable difference is in the 8th last measure.  instead of playing
the !5 harmonics, tap the body of the guitar 4 times (tap, tap-tap, tap)
