Danube Waves (Khasene Vals) - Iosif Ivanovici (Jan Ivanovici, Ion Ivanovici)

This tune might be better known as "Oh How We Danced" or "Anniversary Waltz".
It's a fairly easy piece to play in 3/4 time. It should be played allegretto.
It begins in the key of Am, moves to F and then back to Am.
