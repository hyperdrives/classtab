Tablature Legend
----------------
order of play - measures 1-50, 1-37, 51
sul pont - play close to the bridge
D.C. al Coda - return to the start (Da Capo) and play until "to Coda" (al Coda)

h    - hammer on
L    - tied note
p    - pull off
v    - brush down
x    - dead note
^    - brush up
NH   - natural harmonic
<12> - natural harmonic, open string, pointing to the 12th fret
