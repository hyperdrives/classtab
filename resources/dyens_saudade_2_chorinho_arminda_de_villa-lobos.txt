Saudade No 2: Chorinho, dedicated to Arminda de Villa-Lobos - Roland Dyens
Tablature by Russell Cook (astropig7@gmail.com)

Dyens suggests playing the  16-8-16 rhythm as 16-16-r-16 for authenticity,
where "r" is a sixteenth rest. Also "da S" is my text-only version of "da
segno", and it means jump to the S and play from there.

Tempo: Unspecified
Key: C major
Tuning: Standard (EADGBE)
