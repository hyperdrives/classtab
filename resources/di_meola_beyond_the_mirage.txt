Beyond the Mirage - Al Di Meola (1954-)
[arpeggiation part only]

vid: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZFDJkKane_4 - The Guitar trio
(Paco de Lucia, Al Di Meola, John McLaughlin)

This song is supposed to have three guitars (all played by famous virtuosos),
and go on for much longer, and the one guitar doing this part is supposed to
have 7 strings. In my opinion the arpeggiations are what make the song
mesmerizing and the rest is all just extra. So I like to play this as a
standalone. The 7th string is hardly used anyway. (9 February 2016)
