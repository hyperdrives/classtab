*******************************************************
Lamentos do Morro, by Garoto (Annibal Augusto Sardinha)
*******************************************************

Lamentos do Morro (Sound of the Favelas). The intro imitates the sound of samba
percussions in the favelas. The following melody is a tribute to Ary Barroso's
Aquarela Do Brazil. Original transcription by Paulo Bellinati (1991).
Transcribed from the sheet music by Maxime Bergeron (mazout360@hotmail.com)

video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tizm2SrIrwQ - Paulo Bellinati

tuning: EADGBE   time: 2/4    tempo: 90
