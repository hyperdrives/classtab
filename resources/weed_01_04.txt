Study in Em, Op 1 No 4 - Weed

guitar tab by Weed - weed@wussu.com - http://www.wussu.com
last amended 18 November 2005

The timing is even (no triplets etc). The first note in
each measure (bar) can generally be regarded as a dotted
crotchet, being sustained for the duration of the bar.

This piece was written in 1965. The main theme is very similar
to that used by Symphony X at the start of "Candlelight Fantasy"
from the album "The Divine Wings of Tragedy".


3/8 time   Allegro ma non troppo
