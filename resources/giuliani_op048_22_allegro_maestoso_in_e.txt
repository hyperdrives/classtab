Legend
------
right hand fingering above tab      left hand fingering below tab
p - thumb                           1 - index
i - index index                     2 - middle
m - middle index                    3 - ring
a - annular (ring) finger           4 - little

===   - note held
g     - grace note(s) - measures 2,4,8,17,18 upper mordent (see below)
h     - hammer-on
p     - pull-off
|-3-| - triplet - 3 notes in the time of 2 notes

cII  - part barre on the 2nd fret with the 1st finger
cIV  - part barre on the 4th fret with the 1st finger
cVI  - part barre on the 6th fret with the 1st finger
cIX  - part barre on the 9th fret with the 1st finger
