Legend
------

===    held note
p      pull-off
h      hammer-on
/      slide up
\      slide down
(      arpeggiate - strum from the lowest string to the highest string
)
AH     artificial harmonic
NH     natural harmonic
g      grace note(s)
pizz.  pizzicato
nat.   naturally (negates a previous pizz.)

< - .  crescendo
. - >  decrescendo
/.\    fermata
-      tenuto
.      staccato
+      mezzo staccato
>      accent
^      heavy accent
,      breathe
()     optional note
ff     fortissimo (very loud)
f      forte (loud)
mf     mezzo forte (medium loud)
mp     mezzo piano (medium soft)
p      piano (soft)
pp     pianissimo (very soft)
sub.   subito (suddenly)

maestoso      majestically
leggiero      lightly, delicately
volante       flying
tristamente   sorrowfully
doloroso      painful
dolce         sweetly
dolcissimo    very sweetly
quasi niente  almost nothing (i.e., silence)
calando       falling away

espressivo e ben pronunziato la melodia   expressive and clear melody
sempre pp gli accompagnamenti             always play the accompaniment pianissimo

<5>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 5th fret
<7>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 7th fret
<9>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 9th fret
<12>   natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 12th fret
