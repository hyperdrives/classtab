Legend
------

left hand fingering below the tab
  1   index
  2   middle
  3   ring
  4   little

f      forte (loud)
mf     mezzo forte (medium loud)
mp     mezzo piano (medium soft)
rit.   ritenuto - suddenly slowing down

===    held note
p      pull-off
g      grace notes (extra notes)
/      slide up
/.\    fermata (pause - note held for longer than its value)
.      staccato (note clipped - cut short - played for less than its value)
+      mezzo staccato (note slightly clipped)
>      accent (stressed)
,      breathe (wait before continuing)
cII    partial barre on the 2nd fret
CII    full barre on the 2nd fret
