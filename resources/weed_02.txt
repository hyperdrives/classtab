NOTES

CI   - barre with 1st finger on 1st fret
CIII - barre with 1st finger on 3rd fret
CV   - barre with 1st finger on 5th fret (but see note below re bar 20)
CVI  - barre with 1st finger on 6th fret

arp - arpeggio                      suggested fingering (below the tab)
h3  - hammer on 3rd fret            1  1st (fore) finger
p0  - pull off to open string       2  2nd (index) finger
o   - staccato (brief, not held)    3  3rd (ring) finger
rit - slow down                     4  4th (little) finger (pinkie)

throughout - holding down full chords (even when not all the notes are
             played) can help prevent unwanted resonance - eg in bar 2,
             fingering the 7th fret 5th string (Am) - this may help
             explain some of the right hand fingering

bars 1,3,5 etc - barre only needed on 2nd 3rd and 4th strings (easier)
bars 20,21 - second barre with 4th finger on top 3 strings
bar 24 - heavy hammer
