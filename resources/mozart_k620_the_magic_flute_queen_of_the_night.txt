              FROM MOZART'S THE MAGIC FLUTE: THE QUEEN OF THE NIGHT ARIA
                                 For 4 Guitars

This is a very hard song to play especially because it was composed for
orchestra and voice. The hardest guitar part is the "Gtr I" or Guitar One. You
can t play guitar one unless you have a guitar with at least 24 frets. For a
midi of it go to http://www.aria-database.com/sounds/midi/mflute14a_queen.mid
Good Luck with this cause you re going to need it. Hope you like it!!!!!!!!!!
