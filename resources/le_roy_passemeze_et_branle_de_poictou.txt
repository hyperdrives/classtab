Passemeze et Branle de Poictou - Adrien Le Roy (1520-1598)
----------------------------------------------------------

From: "Jan Tomaszewski" <laertes@interia.pl>
Date: Tue, 10 Jun 2003 19:22:45 +0200

I decided to send in this tab and midi, because I think that this great
composition should be spread. I think that "passemeze" is a kind of
salterello and "branle" is another dance.
It's from the album "Night In Spain Garden" - played by Narciso Yepes.

If you're interested in Le Roy's compositions, please contact me.
