Notes
-----

Tecla Notes
-----------

Lightly, not too fast: it's Andantino grazioso. Again, Andantino means with
movement. As with so many other pieces, practising the bass line alone will
help to clarify the structure. Try singing the bass line.

Again this piece probably has nothing to do with the a finger. Carcassi
doesn't mark the right hand fingering but it is probably pim imi pim pim etc.

It's interesting that again, as in no. 12, the chords are all very close
together. It gives a good nicely structured effect.

This piece uses the ancient and splendid technique of campanelas, where an
open string rings through changing chords, often high up on the fingerboard.
