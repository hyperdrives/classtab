Legend
------

left hand fingering below tab
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===    held note
p      pull-off
h      hammer-on
/      slide up

(      arpeggiate - strum from the lowest string to the highest string
)

< - .  cresc. - crescendo - getting louder
. - >  decrescendo - getting softer
.      staccato
>      accent
ff     fortissimo (very loud)
f      forte (loud)
mf     mezzo forte (medium loud)
mp     mezzo piano (medium soft)
p      piano (soft)

meno mosso - slower
più - quicker
poco a poco - little by little
risoluto - bold, resolute, energetic

<7>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 7th fret
<19>   natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 19th fret
