did a double-take recording, so there is probably no definitively correct
solo version in tab or in sheet - "Although it won't always be technically
possible to hold on to the melody as written, it has been put down like it is
in order to convey the feeling of legato to be aimed at" (from the sheet)

B,A,E,A,E,A - the record has B,A,C#,E,C#,A (which is playable?) -
