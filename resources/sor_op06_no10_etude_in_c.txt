Notes
-----
Editions used - Simrock 9106 (Boije)
              - Deutscher Verlag fur Musik - DVfM 32077

The fingering is taken from the DVfM edition. In places it may be easier
to play using open strings, or by playing lower down the fret board.

Suggested tempo: 100 bpm or faster (quarter notes) for the Moderato
                 66 bpm or slower (quarter notes) for the Maestoso

The Uros Baric video leaves out the repeats, and does not pause between at the
end of the Moderato. The acciaccatura is played as an appogiatura.

Measure 51 - in the sheet the C on the 2nd string is a dotted quarter note -

Legend
------
left hand fingering below tab
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===  - note held
acc. - acciaccatura - an extra note played just before or on the beat and then
       immediately released
F.   - Fermata - pause (the note is held or the rest is extended)
g    - grace note (leading note) - appogiatura - an accented leading note
       splitting the time value of the following principal note
p    - pull-off
