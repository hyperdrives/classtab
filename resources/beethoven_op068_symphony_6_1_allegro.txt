*************************************************************************
Symphony No 6 in F major Op.68 Mvmt I - Allegro ma non troppo, Main theme
by Ludwig van Beethoven
*************************************************************************

My quick arrangement of Beethoven's famous symphony in F major for solo
guitar. I only did the main theme as the following part is much harder to
transcribe properly whilst respecting the original piece at the same time.
Some notes could be skipped to simplify this piece such as the lower octaves
in measure 16 to 28.

Arranged and transcribed from the original sheet music by Maxime Bergeron
(mazout360@hotmail.com).

video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6w-oEQ_QqGA (orchestral version)

Legend below the tab.

tuning: EADGBE               time: 2/4               tempo: Allegro
