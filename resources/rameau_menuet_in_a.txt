AUTHOR: Jean-Philippe Rameau (1683-1764)
SONG: Minuetto I (in A)
TAB: John Kepa Balparda

Thanks to all the ppl who have tabbed all those beautiful songs I've gotten
from this site.  Now this is my change to give my 2 cents to this amazing page.
Really easy song once you get the hang of it (The 16ths hammer-ons and
pull-offs may be a little tricky; they're mainly just embellishment and may be
optional, but it sounds a lot better with the hammer-ons, so it's worth trying).

TAB EXPLANATION:
H = Hammer on or Pull off
/ = Slide
| = Beat

3/4 Time signature
Allegro Grazioso (I personally play it 115 - 130 BPM)
