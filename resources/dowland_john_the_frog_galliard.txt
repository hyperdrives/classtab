The Frog Galliard - John Dowland (1563-1626)

When I heard Scott Tennant play this piece on his video Pumping Nylon,
I had to learn it.  Thanks to Weed for hunting it down for me in a
TablEdit version for lute.  I took that and watched Scott play it a
half million times in order to come up with this arrangement.
