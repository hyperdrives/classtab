Slavonic March in B-flat minor, Op 31 (Marche Slave) - Tchaikovsky

tabbed by Simon Ghyselincks - sghyselincks@gmail.com - July 2010

The first few bars of this piece have 3 ghost notes played
before the 4th note each time.

The section that follows has a lot of rests in it and should
be played like the sound of the footsteps of a marching soldier.

Eventually the gaps get filled in giving a more flowing feel, refer
to the attached MIDI file for reference.
