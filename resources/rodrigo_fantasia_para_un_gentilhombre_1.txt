 Joaquin Rodrigo - Fantasia para un Gentilhombre - 1. Villano y Ricercar

 Tabbed by: The_S

 The_S: This is the piece which began my journey into the realms of classical
        music. I heard it on a mixed-selection classical CD and instantly
        wanted to play the guitar parts. I searched the net for an hour or so,
        and found no trace of a tab, not even a Midi. So I ended up buying the
        Piano and Guitar reduction of this orchestral piece, and have begun
        transcribing it to tabs. Unless you have an orchestra handy, I suggest
        finding a recording of this piece, and play along with that. If you try
        to play it solo, you will find it very boring at parts where the
        orchestra is supposed to be playing. This tab contains the first part of
        the score, called Villano y Ricercar. I will most likely convert more of
        the score to tab, as my interest evolves. Some of the beat measures '|'
        are unevenly spread, which I hate doing, but it is necessary to space
        the tabs out so they are readable.

~Villano~
