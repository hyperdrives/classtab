Title:  Variations on a Theme by Handel op 107,  The Harmonious Blacksmith
Composer:  Mauro Giuliani (1781-1829)
Tabulator:  Donavan Wells (donwells@hotmail.com)

Date:  Nov 17, 2001

"This famous Handel theme with accompanying variations was published in 1720
as the concluding movement of a suite.  It became known as 'The Harmonious
Blacksmith', though this title first appeared nearly a hundred years after the
original publication, and its origin is still unknown. The variations in this
setting do not follow those of Handel and are Giuliani's own composition."
-- from the notes on the sheet music

Tempo = 88 bpm
