In tabbing this song, I broke up each measure of the original score
into four measures. This is because the abundance of thirty-second
and sixty-fourth notes would have required each measure to be far
too long. So, for example, the first chord is not a whole note, but
actually a quarter note. Anyway, it's easier to keep track of the
rhythm when broken down like this. Listen to the MIDI for a suggested
tempo.

Time: 4/4

Tuning: Standard (EADGBE)
