FINAL FANTASY III -- THE OPERA SONG
By Nobuo Uematsu (born 1959)

Arrange by: Simon Poliquin - <poli807@aei.ca>
http://fueled.hypermart.net/
Feb. 26, 2001

For this piece, I have arranged the harp and MIDI voice (Celeb) for guitar,
and it turned out pretty well. So enjoy!

Tuning: EADGBE
Time signature: 4/4
Tempo: 75 bpm
