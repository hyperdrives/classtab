TOCCATA IN A - Pietro Domenico Paradies (1710-1792)

arrangement and guitar tablature by Weed - weed@wussu.com
revised 4 January 2002

MIDI file available from the Classical Guitar Tablature page

the piece can be divided into 2 sections - the division point is
halfway through measure (bar) 29 - each section can be repeated

in some editions, the melody in the 2nd half of measure 29 (BEG#E)
is repeated in the 2nd half of measure 31 (rather than EBG#E)

the doubled octaves in bars 65-69 are not present in all
editions - a simpler version of this section is appended

barees (CI etc) above the tab - many of the barres are not full,
and some use the 4th finger - left-hand fingering below the tab

the tempo of the piano edition is a lively Allegro (116 bpm);
George Malcolm's scintillating harpsichord recording whizzes
along at approx 150 bpm, twice the suggested speed for guitar
