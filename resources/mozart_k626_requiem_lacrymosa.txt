Lacrymosa - W A Mozart (1719-1787)

I first heard this for guitar by Gerry Gianutsos, and it blew me away. I've
always liked this peice. This is for two guitars. I did this mostly by ear 
and looking at the music for 1st and 2nd violins. If anyone has any 
suggestions on parts of this let me know. <tk421jag@hotmail.com>. Some of 
the chords are a stretch, but if you've been playing for a while they 
shouldn't be too much of a problem. Timing between the two guitars is very 
important, use a metronome for accuracy. If fingering is needed, let me know 
and I'll repost it.

              LACRYMOSA FROM MOZARTS REQUIEM MASS IN DM
                       As written by Mozart

Arranged by Jason Glisson
Transcribed by Jason Glisson
