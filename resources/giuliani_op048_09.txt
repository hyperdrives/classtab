A very funny studio, certainly not for beginners yet playable.
Like with studio op.6, the MIDI is simplified, i.e. some measures which
are played twice in the sheet music are missing; the omitted measures
are indicated in the tab.

Don't forget to select Courier font to view the tab in the best way,
and see my Variazioni op.112 tab if you need explanations about
musical notation.
