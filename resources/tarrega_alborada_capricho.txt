Legend & Notes
--------------
"The triplets are played by the left hand alone, all on the first string.
The right hand plays the artificial harmonics. Technique: 1st finger on
the fret, 3rd finger plucks. In the second part, it is best to play the
first 4 triplets on the first string, the next 2 on the second, the
final D on the 3rd." - Klaus Heim
More info on rec.music.classical.guitar - http://tinyurl.com/omq8ygt

measure 10 - in an older edition the last 4 notes are C# A F# C# (it is
             possible that all four notes have been wrongly placed two
             notes below on the printed stave, as the F# is wrong); in
             newer editions the last 4 notes are usually E C# A E which
             follows the pattern in earlier measures; the last 4 notes
             are sometimes played C# A E C# (as in the vid)

order of play - measures 0-16, 1-15, 17-33, 1-16

h    - hammer on
p    - pull off
s    - slide
-3-  - triplet (3 notes played in the time of 2 notes)
<>   - natural harmonics
