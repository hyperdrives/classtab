Notes & Legend
--------------

The lute tuning was probably in G, equivalent to using a capo on the 3rd fret.
Zamboni's pieces are sometimes played with the 3 bottom strings tuned an octave
lower. The guitar sheet fingering tries to reflect the original lute music.

The MIDI file is 66 bpm (quarter notes).

measure 18 - the sheet has all the melody notes played on the 2nd string -

measure 18 -  in the sheet, the B on the 2nd beat (5th string, 2nd fret) is a
half note, but it is not followed by a rest, so it should probably be a dotted
half note (following the pattern of measure 1)

h    - hammer on
p    - pull-off
