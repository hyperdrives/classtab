Duration Legend
---------------
H - half
Q - quarter
E - 8th
S - 16th
T - 32nd
. - note dotted
|-n-|  - n-tuplets

Tablature Legend
----------------
L - tied note
g - grace note
h - hammer on
p - pull off
~ - vibrato
S - Slide
^ - brush up
v - brush down

AH   - artificial harmonic
<4>  - artificial harmonic - 4th fret, octave harmonic
<5>  - artificial harmonic - 5th fret, octave harmonic
<7>  - artificial harmonic - 7th fret, octave harmonic

NH - natural harmonic
<12> - natural harmonic - open string, octave harmonic
