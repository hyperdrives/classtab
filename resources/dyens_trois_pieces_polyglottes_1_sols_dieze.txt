****************************
Sols d'Ièze, by Roland Dyens
****************************

Sols d'Ièze, phonetically equivalent to sol dièse or G sharp, is a
piece demanded from Roland Dyens after he told Danielle Ribouillault,
the founder of "Les Cahiers de la Guitare" magazine, that his favorite
note was G sharp. He confirmed that next time, he'll choose A instead.

Transcribed from the sheet music by Maxime Bergeron (mazout360@hotmail.com).

Video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2Miz1AW79tU (Roland Dyens)

See the Legend below the tab.

tuning: EADGBE (variable)   time: variable    tempo: con calma, libero

Duration Legend
---------------
H     - half note
Q     - quarter note
E     - 8th note
S     - 16th note
T     - 32nd note
.     - dotted note
|-3-| - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes

Tablature Legend
----------------
arp. - arpeggiate - ripple upwards
g    - grace note
h    - hammer on
L    - tied note
p    - pull off
v    - brush down
\    - slide into from above or out of downwards
AH   - Artificial Harmonic
NH   - Natural Harmonic
