Duration Legend
---------------

Tablature Legend
----------------
 h   - hammer-on
 p   - pull-off
 s   - legato slide
 S   - shift slide
<n>  - natural harmonic
[n]  - artificial harmonic
 =   - hold bend; also acts as connecting device for hammers/pulls/slides
