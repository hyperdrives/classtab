Legend
------

left hand fingering below tab
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===    held note
g      grace note
p      pull-off

..3..  triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
< - .  crescendo - becoming louder
. - >  decrescendo - becoming softer
/.\    fermata - pause
-      (above the tab) tenuto - held for at least its full value
>      accent
,      breathe (pause)
V___   barre on the 5th fret

dim.        diminuendo (getting gradually softer)
f           forte (loud)
ff          fortissimo (very loud)
mf          mezzo forte (medium loud)
p           piano (soft)
pp          pianissimo (very soft)
rall.       rallentando (slowing down)
sul tasto   play above the fingerboard
