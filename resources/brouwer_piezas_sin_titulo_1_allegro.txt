Legend
------

===      - held note
p        - pull-off
h        - hammer-on
/        - slide up
-        - tenuto
.        - staccato
>        - accent

(        - arpeggiate - strum from the lowest string to the highest string
)

- - >    - decrescendo (becoming softer)
,        - breathe (pause)
a tempo  - return to the original tempo
cantando - in a singing style
dim.     - diminuendo (getting gradually softer)
dolce    - sweetly
f        - forte (loud)
marcato  - marked, accented, stressed
metalico - metallic, of a metallic quality
mp       - mezzo piano (medium soft)
p        - piano (soft)
pp       - pianissimo (very soft)
pizz.    - pizzicato (plucked)
rit.     - ritardando / ritenuto - slowing down gradually / slowing down suddenly
ritmico  - rhythmical
sf       - sforzando (forced, strongly accented, suddenly loud)
sfz      - sforzando (forced, strongly accented, suddenly loud)
sub.     - subito (suddenly)

II___    - barre on the 2nd fret
IV___    - barre on the 4th fret
V____    - barre on the 5th fret
