Performance notes
* The fingering in this tab differs wildly from the fingering in the sheet
  music in several measures. The preparatory material in the collection of
  studies notes the indicated fingering may be unexpected, but in some cases
  I find it to be unnecessary work for little, if any, gain.

===    held note
p      pull-off
h      hammer-on
~      vibrato

ff     fortissimo (very loud)
f      forte (loud)
mf     mezzo forte (medium loud)
mp     mezzo piano (medium soft)
p      piano (soft)
pp     pianissimo (very soft)
sub.   subito (suddenly)
rall.  rallentando (slow down)

poco a poco   little by little

<5>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 5th fret
<7>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 7th fret
<9>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 9th fret
<12>   natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 12th fret
[5]    artificial harmonic - play indicated fret, touch point on 17th fret
