Opus 24 - Variations on "Three Blind Mice"
by John W. Duarte
based on the English nursery rhyme and musical round
first published in Guitar Revue No 25
Tablature by Russell Cook (astropig7@gmail.com)

Each variation is written in the style of another composer. I've preceded each
variation with the composer's name and the type of song Duarte wished to invoke.

In variation 2, there is a repeat in the middle of measure 10. This equally
divides the measure into three eighth notes, but I've kept the timing as if it
were still three quarter notes.

Key: C major
Tuning: Standard (EADGBE)
