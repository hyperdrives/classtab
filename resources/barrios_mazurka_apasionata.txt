Mazurka Apasionata (Impassioned Mazurka)
Agustin Barrios Mangore (1885 - 1944)

Tablature by: David McGirr - davidmcgirr@hotmail.com
              http://www.geocities.com/mcgirr16

In my opinion this is one of Barrios's great Masterpieces. It is written in
a Chopin-like Mazurka and also like the Appassionata which Beethoven wrote.
I think Barrios wrote this quite late in his life as he never recorded it,
though if you would like to hear a recording try -
  John Williams - "The Great Paraguayan"
  David Russell - "David Russell plays Barrios"

I reckon the 10th bar is the longest stretch I've seen.
Enjoy - Good Luck!

Key: C
Tuning: EADGBE
