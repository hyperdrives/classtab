Notes and Legend
----------------
The time signature of the sheet music is 3/4 (to emphasise that it is a waltz),
but the sheet music is written as though it were 3/8, which usually means that
the piece is to be played at a fast rather than a slow tempo. The tempo of the
MIDI is 128 bpm (eighth notes).

=  - note held
.  - staccato - detached - the note is cut short (not played its full length)
g  - grace note (acciaccatura) - the following note is an pull off
h  - hammer on
p  - pull off
sf - sforzando - forced, strongly accented
