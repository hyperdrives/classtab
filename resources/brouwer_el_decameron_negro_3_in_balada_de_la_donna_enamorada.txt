El Decameron Negro - 3. In Balada De La Doncella Enamorada
(Ballade de la Demoiselle Amoureuse)
by Leo Brouwer

Tabbed by Scott Erickson - axeman1033@aol.com

See key at bottom for notes on techniques.

This is a great song, but extremely difficult.  The time signature changes
a lot.  The timing for this song is fairly rigid with some parts that can be
played loosely.  There are a lot of hard stretches and open string tricks.

6th string tuned to D
