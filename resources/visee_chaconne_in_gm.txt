Robert de Visée - Chaconne in G minor
(from the Saizenay manuscript, page 188)

Play slowly and add trills as much as you can, to match the french lute
style of the 17th century, though you are free to play it the way you want.

Dropped D tuning. If you want the guitar pro file of this piece,
feel free to contact me.

Author and contact : Maxime PIERRE (maximepierre@free.fr) 10/2014
