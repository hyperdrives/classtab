Notes and Legend
----------------

the tempo of the MIDI is 52 bpm (dotted quarter notes)

===  - note held
>    - accented, stressed
g    - grace note - acciaccatura - the note is played immediately before or
       at the same time as the following note, and immediately released
h    - hammer-on
p    - pull-off

cI   - partial barre on the 1st fret with the 1st finger
cII  - partial barre on the 2nd fret with the 1st finger
cIII - (measure 10) partial barre on the 3rd fret with the 4th finger
CIII - barre on the 3rd fret with the 1st finger
