===    held note
p      pull-off
h      hammer-on

< - -  crescendo (growing louder)
- - >  decrescendo (growing softer)
/.\    fermata - pause
-      (above the tab) tenuto (note held, emphasised)
.      (above the tab) staccato (detached, clipped)
+      mezzo staccato
>      note accented, stressed
,      breathe (slight pause)
f      forte (loud)
mf     mezzo forte (medium loud)
p      piano (soft)
pp     pianissimo (very soft)
sf     sforzando (forced, strongly accented, suudenly loud)
sub.   subito (suddenly)
