This is an interesting studio for several aspects. Apart from inventiveness
and beaty of the sound, usual in Giuliani, this is one of the few in op.48
where he uses some ornaments (acciaccatura); furthermore in two of the central
measures the highest note playable on a classical guitar is used, the B at the
19th fret of the first string.

MAURO GIULIANI (BISCEGLIE 1781 - NAPOLI 1829)

Time :4/4
Signature: A major
Tuning: Standard
Date: October 15, 2004

Allegro
