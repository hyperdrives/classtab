                     Gnossienne No. 2
                    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
                        Erik Satie.

Arranged for guitar by Jasmine Korcok (jkorcok@uoguelph.ca),
17 June 2004.
I found this to be the most difficult Satie piece to tab, partly
because I could not find sheet music with stave indications or
a time signature (perhaps it does not exist).
Consequently, it is the hardest to play of the Trois Gnossiennes,
but I have done my best to make the tab legible.
Distance between each hash mark represents a quarter note.
Melody notes distinguished by *.
Lent et avec étonnement.
