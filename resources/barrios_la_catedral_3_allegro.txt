The previous line of tab can be played in two ways. There is
the "easy" version (On top) and the "better sounding but harder"
version (below). You can play whatever way you like, or you can
even alternate both when you repeat the song. Also, the most common
way you'll hear it on a record is the one on the top, i've only
heard the one below by John Williams in a really old record.
