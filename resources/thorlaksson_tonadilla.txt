Notes and Legend
----------------

Allegro - fairly fast

play in the following order - measures 1-48, 1-16, 49-56
CODA - the end section of the piece
D.C. - Da Capo (go back to the beginning and repeat)
al CODA - jump to the CODA (on the repeat)

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index
2 - middle
3 - ring
4 - little

===  note held
h    hammer-on
p    pull-off
/    slide left hand finger up to the next note
CII  barre with the first finger on the 2nd fret
