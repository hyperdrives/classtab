Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
The sheet does not show the left hand fingering. Feel free to improve it. 
The tempo of the MIDI is 40 bpm (quarter notes).
(The tempo is slower in the Eliot Fisk / Chiara Morandi video.)
Please email if you want the sheet music for the violin part - weed@wussu.com

====   - note held, length of note (duration)
F.     - Fermata - pause (rest)
g      - grace note - acciacatura - played as hammer-on or pull-off at the same
         time as, or just before the following note and immediately released
h      - hammer-on
p      - (between the notes) pull-off
|-5-|  - pentuplet - 5 notes in the time of 4 notes
|-6-|  - sextuplet - 6 notes in the time of 4 notes
cresc. - crescendo - growing louder
dolce  - sweetly, gently, expressively
f      - (below the tab forte - loud
p      - (below the tab) piano - soft
rf     - rinforzato - accented
