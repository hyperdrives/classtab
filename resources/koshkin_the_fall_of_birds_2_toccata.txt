Legend
------

left hand fingering below tab
1 - index
2 - middle
3 - ring
4 - little

<   crescendo, getting louder
>   decrescendo, getting softer
=   note held
^   arpeggiate, ripple upwards (from 6th to 1st string)
h   hammer on
p   pull off
s   slide right hand finger to the next note

CI    - barre on the 1st fret
CII   - barre on the 2nd fret
CIII  - barre on the 3rd fret
CV    - barre on the 5th fret
CVII  - barre on the 7th fret
