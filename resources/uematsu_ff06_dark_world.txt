Dark World:  Final Fantasy 6 soundtrack  (Nobuo Uematsu)

The first 12 measures are a verbatim transcription of the song (the one played
when the world is transformed and Celes is stranded on the island).  I added
the same thing in arpeggios for the next twelve measures because this is what
the piano arrangement of this piece might have looked like had they chosen to
include it the FFVI piano collection (which sadly they did not).  I think this
is an accurate, if simple, transcription.  It's my first computer tab.  A few
of the chord progressions are difficult, since most notes should ring out as
much as possible, but a little practice and flexible hands can pull it off no
problem.  Pay special attention to the obvious melody notes.  I also made up an
ending, since the original version did not have one.

If you any other suggestions for FF pieces that are suitable for guitar
email me at monstrosity33@hotmail.com.

Tabbed by:  Joshua Serrano
