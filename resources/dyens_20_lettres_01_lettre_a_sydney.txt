Legend

right hand fingering above tab      left hand fingering below tab
p - thumb                           1 - index
i - index                           2 - middle
m - middle                          3 - ring
a - ring                            4 - little

==  - shows duration (length) of note
/   - (below the tab) move left hand finger up to play the next note
\   - (below the tab) move left hand finger down to play the next note
h   - hammer on

< < < - crescendo (gradually becoming louder)
> > > - decrescendo (gradually becoming softer)
