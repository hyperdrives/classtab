3 Delineations - 1. Lullaby - Andrew York (born 1958)

measures 13,16,29,30 corrected - Dave Kliewer, Feb 2003

additional corrections made per original score / CD - jim shea 12/2004

"There is a section (bars 30-32) where Andrew York plays the melody with
RH harmonics, very tricky but beautiful and worth the effort. While this
is actually not notated in the score, he plays it this way on the CD and
also when he performs it live. York has mastered RH harmonics and can
produce them with almost any combination of his RH fingers. For this
piece he uses the M to damp and the A to pluck, or at least that is what
he did when I saw him perform it." - jim shea
