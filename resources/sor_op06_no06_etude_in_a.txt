NOTES (by KJM)

I'd recommend that you attack this piece visually. By that, I mean learn
eight measures at a time, then visualize your left-hand fingering as it
appears on the fretboard. Kind of like memorizing a phone number by how
the number pattern looks on the keyboard. Start out slowly. Once you have
a phrase memorized without mistakes, look at the next eight measures.
You'll pick up speed as you go. This ia a moderately easy place to play
in that there are no contorted fingerings and no jumping up and down the
keyboard. The key is in the memorization. Once mastered, it will make a
great addition to your repertoire.

tuning: EADGBE  -  key: A major  -  time: 3/8
