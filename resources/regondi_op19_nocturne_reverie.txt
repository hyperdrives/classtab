Notes
-----
Measure 8: In older sheet editions (Boije/Offenbach, GFA/RiBS/Krick, Bone) the
first chord appears to be E B# C G (which would make the 7th note B#) - this
looks like a mistake. In the later Chiesa/Suivini Zerboni edition, the first
chord is E Bb C# G (as in the tab) and so the 7th note is C#.
Thanks to anaxiphorminges for spotting this. - weed@wussu.com
