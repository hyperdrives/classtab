Duration Legend
---------------
H - half note
Q - quarter note
E - 8th note
S - 16th note
T - 32nd note 
. - dotted note
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
|-4-|  - quintuplet - 5 notes played in the time of 4 notes
|-6-|  - triplet - 6 notes played in the time of 4 notes

Tablature Legend
----------------
h - hammer on
L - tied note
p - pull off
s - slide
/ -  slide into from below or out of upwards
^ - brush up
> - accented (with staccato)

NH - natural harmonic
<7>  - natural (open) harmonic on the 7th fret
<12> - natural (open) harmonic on the 12th fret
<19> - natural (open) harmonic on the 19th fret
