PAGANINI: MS 2, Op 61, SONATE CONCERTATE - 1st MOVEMENT: Allegro spiritoso

This is the guitar part for the first movement of the Sonata for Guitar and
Violin in A. The violin and guitar assume pretty much equal roles, and the
piece could possibly work as a duet for two guitars.
