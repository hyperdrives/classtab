Author/Artist: F. Carulli (1770-1841)
Title: Allegretto in C
Transcribed by David Magalhães (faticata@mail.pt)

This is a simple piece. It´s easy and has some good happy-melancolic moods.
Left-hand is present in the piece. The right-hand is also present, and the
" ^ ". The tempo is at your own description (80 beats...).
