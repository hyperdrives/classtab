BWV 1009, Cello Suite No 3 in C - 1. Prelude - Johann Sebastian Bach (1685-1750)

I believe it was November. A Russian classical guitarist named Vladimir,
played this piece early one morning in the streets of the Norwegian capital
Oslo some years ago.  Not only did he play the piece beautifully, but it was
damn cold that morning! I'm sure his guitar neck must have bent 78 degrees
that evening!

Anyway, it's a great piece, good luck!

Roger Margido Proeis
Classical Guitarist
margido@live.hk

This piece should be played evenly throughout, save for the beginning and
the end.  I suggest you listen to this song as played by Andres Segovia -
it's beautiful.

Tablature revised by Michael Karr - February 2002 - Gandalf514@hotmail.com
(orginal tab by Roger Margido Proeis)
