Notes & Legend
--------------

The lute tuning was probably in G, equivalent to using a capo on the 3rd fret.
Zamboni's pieces are sometimes played with the 3 bottom strings tuned an octave
lower. The guitar sheet fingering tries to reflect the original lute music.

The Satita Sae-Tang St John video uses standard tuning. The piece has been
transposed to F and is played with a capo on the 1st fret, so it sounds like F#.
The Luciano Contini video is in the key of Bb.
The Sergio Pes video is in the ket of G.

Suggested tempo - 54 bpm (quarter notes)

measure 16 - the sheet has the 1st 2 notes as a dotted half then an eighth note
measure 21 - the sheet has the E# on the 2nd beat 1st string as a half note
measure 24 - the sheet has the F# on the 1st beat as a half note

===    - note held
h      - hammer-on
p      - pull-off
8      - (below the tab) additonal bass note one octave lower in the lute sheet
##     - an ornament is indicated in the original
