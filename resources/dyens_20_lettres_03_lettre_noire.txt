Legend

right hand fingering above tab      left hand fingering below tab
p - thumb                           1 - index
i - index                           2 - middle
m - middle                          3 - ring
a - ring                            4 - little

p (between notes) - pull off
h (between notes) - hammer on
s (between notes) - slide
g (between notes) - grace note
. (under notes) - staccato (as short as possible)

/ - slide up
\ - slide down

<<<<< - crescendo (gradually becoming louder)
>>>>> - decrescendo (gradually becoming softer)
rall. - getting slower
fe - fermata (stop longer than written)
lentissimo - very slow

measure 24 + 33: \ - slide the chord slowly down
