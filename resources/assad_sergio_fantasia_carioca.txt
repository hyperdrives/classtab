Tablature Legend
----------------
g  - grace note
h  - hammeer on
L  - tied note
LH - played with the left hand only
p  - pull off
s  - slide
tr - trill
>  - accented note

AH    - artificial harmonic
3<15> - left hand 3rd fret, right hand points to the 15th fret
4<16> - left hand 4th fret, right hand points to the 16th fret

NH    - natural harmonic
<7>   - open string, right hand points to the 7th fret
<12>  - open string, right hand points to the 12th fret
<19>  - open string, right hand points to the 19th fret
