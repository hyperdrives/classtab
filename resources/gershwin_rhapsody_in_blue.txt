Legend:

* - accelerating, alternating hammer-ons and pull-offs using right & left hands
X:Y*Z - X Z-notes are equal to Y Z-notes
        (sixteen 32nd notes are equal to eight 32nd notes in bar 1)
W, H, Q, E, S, T - whole, half, quarter, eight, sixteenth, thirty-second notes
R - rest
a - acciaccatura (note taking up "no time")
/ - slide up
\ - slide down
<x> - harp or natural harmonic (played by lightly touching the string while
      playing, 12 frets up from x)
