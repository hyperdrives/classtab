Notes and Legend
----------------

The lute tuning was probably in G, equivalent to using a capo on the 3rd fret.
It is sometimes played with the three bottom strings tuned one octave lower.

Legend
------

===    - note held
h      - hammer-on
p      - pull-off
8      - (below the tab) additonal bass note one octave lower in the lute sheet
##     - an ornament is indicated in the original
#?     - possibly vibrato
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
