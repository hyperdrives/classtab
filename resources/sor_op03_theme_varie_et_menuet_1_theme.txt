notes
-----

Edition used - Simrock 2288 (Boije 487)
The Stich and Druck (9103) edition has two triplets instead of a sextuplet in
measure 13. The Meissonnier edition is the same as the Simrock edition.
In the Castro edition (Journal de Musique Étrangère) the ornaments (grace notes)
are different, and there are no staccato notes.

measure 13 - an extra note (G) is inserted before the A in the Castro edition -

legend
------

===     - note held
__3__   - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
__6__   - sextuplet - 6 notes (including the rest) played in the time 4 notes
.       - staccato - detached - not held for full value - slightly emphasised
g       - grace note - played immediately before or at the same time as the
          following note and then released
h       - hammer on
p       - pull off
s       - slide
