BWV 1001 - Violin Sonata No 1 in Gm - 1. Adagio - Johann Sebastian Bach

Dramatic and intense piece of music. For the preferable sound, tune your
classical piece of wood to "D G C F A D". But of course, you may play
"E A D G B E" as well. Give the piece a spiritual intense feeling, good luck!
