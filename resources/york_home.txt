Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the MIDI file is 76 bpm (quarter notes).

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===    - note held, note duration, length of note
-      - (above the tab) tenuto - the note is held for at least its full length,
         or a little longer, marked, stressed
,      - (above the tab) - staccato - cut short, abrupt, detached
/      - (below the tab) slide up
< < <  - (below the tab) crescendo - growing louder
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
F.     - Fermata - pause, note(s) held
g      - grace note (ornament) - in the sheet these are shown as appoggiaturas
         (passing notes), extra note(s) leading toward the main note, sometimes
         played as hammer-ons, pull-offs, or slides
h      - hammer-on
mp     - mezzopiano - medium soft
p      - pull-off
<12>   - natural (octave) harmonic on the 12th fret
cII    - part barre on the 2nd fret
CII    - barre on the 2nd fret

a tempo - return to the original tempo
poco rit. - poco ritenuto - a little slower
rit. - slowing down
