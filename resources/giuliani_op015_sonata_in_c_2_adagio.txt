Duration Legend
---------------
Q       - quarter note
E       - 8th note
S       - 16th note
T       - 32nd note
X       - 64th note
.       - dotted note
|-3-|   - triplet - 3 notes in the time of 2 notes
|-10-|  - decuplet - 10 notes in the time of 8 notes

Tablature Legend
----------------
arp. - arpeggiate - ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest note
g    - grace note  - acciaccatura - the note is played immediately before
       or at the same time as the following note, and immediately released
h    - hammer on
L    - tied note
p    - pull off
