            Title: Pavane No.2 (from "Seis Pavanes da el Maestro")
           Author: Luis de Milan  (1500 - 1561 / Renaissance)
   Transcribed by: Adam Guzowski (guitarra@poczta.fm)
             Date: 10-XII-2002
Other corrections: (...are welcome)

1) Guitar tuning: (standard) EAGDBE - Old lute music sounds good with Capo
                                      on 3rd or 4th fret

2) General Info: This is the 2nd Pavane from the renaissance lute master
                 Luis de Milan. With this one, I'll try to transcribe all
                 6 pavanas from "Seis Pavanes da el Maestro". This one is
                 rather easy. All notes are; full -half or quarter notes.
                 Only in one place are 2 16th notes. This Pavane should be
                 played, soft and calm. All chords should be played very
                 'warm', all single notes in chords, should be heard clearly..
                 Try to hold bass notes in chords, as long as additional
                 notes are ended. Listen to the MIDI... Good luck ! :)

3) Tablature info:

   1 2 3 4  -> left hand fingering
   (I)      -> Hold BARRE Chord (on exp. 1st fret)
   Arp.     -> Arpeggio (all chords can be played with soft arpeggio)
   =====    -> 16th notes
   ,,,,,    -> Try to hold bass note as long as possible (to the end of ,,,)
   (!)      -> Here is 'accent', play this note(-s) chord, a little more 'fore'


Tempo: 4/4  (prox. 145/160 bpm)
