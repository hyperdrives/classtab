From: kevins@ornews.intel.com (Kevin Smith) [email no longer active - May 2012]

Last week someone asked for the tab of the Bach Prelude BWV 999
(Praelude in C moll pour la Lute di J S Bach).  It is one of Bach's
most popular pieces for guitar.  It was originally in Cm, but here it
is transposed to Dm for guitar.  I like to play it brisk and flowing
with a slight accent on the base notes.  It is in 3/4 time; the |'s
represent 8th notes.  Let me know if you find any mistakes.
