Notes
-----

Schott Edition (1853?) - Boije 94 (1924/526)
http://boije.statensmusikverk.se

Eythor Thorlaksson, The Guitar School, Iceland (2000)
http://www.classicalguitarschool.net

"One of the longer pieces, this again goes on and on (with only a pause
at bar 8 and a slight breather at bar 20). The pattern begins to change
at bar 28, especially with the up-ending of the right hand pattern at
bars 31 and 35. To practise it, take it apart first. Play the bass line
alone, then the top line alone. Then those two lines together. Only then
add the repeated notes in the middle."
Tecla edition notes - http://www.tecla.com/extras/0001/0345/notes.htm

sforzando (sf) and rinzando (rf) are probably meant to apply to just the
single note (rather than the phrase)
