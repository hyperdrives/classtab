Legend

===    held note
p      pull-off
h      hammer-on
/      slide up
g      grace note(s)
< - .  crescendo
. - >  decrescendo
.      staccato
<      accent
,      slight pause

(
)      arpeggiate - strum from the lowest string to the highest string
(
)

f      forte (loud)
ff     fortissimo (very loud)
fff    extremely loud
mp     mezzo piano (medium soft)
p      piano (soft)

aigo pesante     play the high notes heavily
allarg           allargando - broadening, becoming slower
dolce cantando   sweetly singing (literally)
cresc            crescendo - gradually getting louder
molto dim        becoming much softer
Piu mosso        more movement
pesante          play heavily
