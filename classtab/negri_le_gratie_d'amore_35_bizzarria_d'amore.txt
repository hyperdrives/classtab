Le Gratie d'Amore - 35. Bizzarria d'Amore - Cesare Negri (1536-1605)

edited by: Bart T. (bla_1024@hotmail.com), 10/2014

"La Musica della sonata con l'Intavolatura di liuto
 del Ballo detto Bizzarria d'Amore. Sono tre parti,
 & si fanno due volte per parte, sino al fin del Ballo."

Please, note the alternative repeat signs in measures 0, 8, 12 and 16.
Renaissance music was often composed in phrases without the strict time notation
that we are used to now. I decided to write the music  down in phrases too (one
phrase per line). This lead to the alternative repeat signs in bars 0, 8, 12
and 16, where the upbeats of bars 1, 9 and 13 are included within the repeat
signs. When playing this dance, the first notes of the bars should remain in a
steady rhythm though.
