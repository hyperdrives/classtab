Important stuff about this piece:
 -All notes are half-quarter notes. The few exceptions are
  noted by the tempo marks (|) above the tablature. This
  exceptions are: The introduction, the end of Part 1,
  the end of part 2, and the end of part 3.
 -My sheet music didnt have fingerings, so I'll put what I
  believe is the best fingering (Look for the fingering under
  the tab). Feel free to do it however you like.
 -I don't like to repeat part 2, however, the original sheet
  music indicates that part has to be repeated. So, repeat it
  if you like. Also, i don't like to play the intro again after
  part 2, so again, play it if you like.
 -There is a BIG stretch at the end of part 2. It's not an error
  in the tab. Don't worry, it comes out easy with practice.
 -Tablature explanation is at the end of the file.
 -Remember that the left hand fingering is:
        1  Index Finger
        2  Middle Finger
        3  Ring Finger
        4  Pinky

[corrections to Part 2 1st measure, and Part 3 2nd to last measure
 by Chris Tisdale - for details see note at the bottom of the tab]

Note - "On the Las Abejas(The Bees) the 2nd Part, seventh note
on that first measure it should be a G, third fret 1st string not
a G#. And on the second to last measure in the whole piece, the
chord that follows the open D should have the notes A  - 5th fret
1st string, G - 8th fret second string, C# - 6th fret 3rd string,
and last the open A - 5th string. The only thing you had wrong was
the F#- 7th fret 2nd string instead of the 8th fret 2nd string- G"
- Chris Tisdale
