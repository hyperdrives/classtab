LEGNANI : CAPRICE NO 7 IN A, OP 20


This is a very nice song from the 36 Caprices of Legnani, very light, lots
of fun!! But make sure that you begin by playing it slowly, slowly, slowly
before thinking about playing it very fast (prestissimo).
Because of errors I may have made tabbing this song, please listen to a midi
file (but take care on the net that it is the CORRECT midi file ...)
Some measures need the use of a metronome.

I have divided this tab into 4 sections - A, B, C, D
These are repeated in the order -  A A B B C C D D A B
You can learn them separately.

GOOD LUCK !!!
