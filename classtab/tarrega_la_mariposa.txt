Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the Vazha Kalandadze video goes up to 220 bpm (eighth notes).
The tempo of the EM Robert video is about 184 bpm (eighth notes).
The left hand fingering attempts to follow the Stretta Music (2004) edition.

left hand fingering below tab
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===   - note held
h     - hammer on
p     - (between notes) pull off
\     - slide down
CVII  - barre on the 7th fret with the 1st finger
CXII  - barre on the 12th fret with the 1st finger
