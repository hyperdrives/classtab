This is my second stab at doing a tab and I must admit, after down loading
"Bucket O'tab" (a superb must-have program) it was a thousand times easier
- since I did my first tab, "Nelly" by A.Lauro, manually.

This is a brilliant piece by Tarrega, that I only came across last year 
when I bought a CD by Norbert Kraft, and its one of them instantly
likeable pieces, that you say to yourself I must buy the music and learn
the piece. Anyway here it is.....
Enjoy!!!!!!!!! 

**************************************************************************
Take note of the slides as these must be really emphasised. :-)
The hammer ons in bar4 are also real tricky.
