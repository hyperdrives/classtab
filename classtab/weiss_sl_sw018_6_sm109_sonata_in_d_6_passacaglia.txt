Sonata in D - 6. Passacaglia (Passacaille) - Sylvius Leopold Weiss (1687-1750)
WeissSW 18.6, Sm 109, Klima 156

Tabbed by Jonathan Barchas - solifugyd(@T)gmail(d.t)com

This beautiful piece is of intermediate difficulty. The double bars every 8
measures indicate where each section ends. The sheet music I have tells you
to repeat at the end of this section, but this makes for a rather long and
unwieldy piece. I deviated from the sheet music to bring the piece closer to
Julian Bream's rendition of it, which shouldn't be too hard to find. If only
playing by sight or by the MIDI, however, keep in mind that the tempo is not
constant throughout the piece, and that it's extremely slow at the beginning
until the fourth section, at which point things speed up a bit.

Don't forget to drop the D. Also, let the bass notes ring as long as possible.
