Choral de G.F. Haendel (1685-1759) (transcribed by Tarrega)

Beautiful piece. It's not too hard. Play slowly a tempo.
You can slow down in the end and play second time of each part louder.
Or you can play crescendo all along, as you feel it. Any combination
is possible. So have fun! - yannickroulin at yahoo dot com
