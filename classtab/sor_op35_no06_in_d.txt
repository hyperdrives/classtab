Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the Max Coradduzza video and the MIDI is 84 bpm (quarter notes).
The Max Coradduzza video repeats measures 1-8, then repeats measures 9-24.

measure 13 - in the Boije edition the last interval is D# G# (not D G#)

2 - middle finger

        as a pull-off immediately  before or at the same time as the following
        note, and immediately released
