Bouree Alla Polacca - Georg Philipp Telemann (1681-1767)

Tabbed by: Dave Shering

Like 'La Volta' (Praetorius), this song too is from John Williams'
1978 'Best of' album. It's played with a xylophone accompaniment,
but this transcription has all parts on guitar (just as well really!).
It's a nice one to play to cheer you up, and just 'feels good' to play.
Keep a steady pace throughout.

2/4 timing
 ~90 bpm
