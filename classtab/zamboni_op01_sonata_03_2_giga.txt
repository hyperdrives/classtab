Notes
-----

The lute tuning was probably in G, equivalent to using a capo on the 3rd fret.
Zamboni's pieces are sometimes played with the 3 bottom strings tuned an octave
lower. The guitar sheet fingering tries to reflect the original lute music.
Some of the stretches and held notes show the composer's intentions, but are not
the easiest way to play them on a guitar, and in some cases are hardly possible,
especially while holding the bass notes (for example measures 55-57).

Measure 14 - the sheet has the E on the 1st beat as a dotted half note.
