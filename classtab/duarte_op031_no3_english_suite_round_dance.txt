Tuning: Standard 

  w = whole note            W = dotted whole
w+e = 4 & 1/2 
  h = half note             H = dotted half
h+e = 2 & 1/2
  q = quarter note          Q = dotted quarter
  e = eighth note           E = dotted eighth
  s = sixteenth note        S = dotted sixteenth 
  r = eight rest            R = quarter rest
 RR = half Rest             . = dotted rest (when placed after r,R,RR)
  F = sixteenth rest
 FR = 1 & 1/16 rest       RRr = 2 & 1/2 beat rest
