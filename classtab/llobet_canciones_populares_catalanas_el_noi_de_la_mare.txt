Catalonian Song (anon) - arranged Jack Marshall

This anonymous piece, a Catalan folksong has a few tabbings
available to it. However, arrangements by Jack Marshall are
quite beautiful and melodic. I am sure you will agree this
version is quite different from the other two I have seen.

Note the tuning: D-A-D-G-B-E

Paul R. Estrada - November 2002
estrada@cosmic.arc.nasa.gov
