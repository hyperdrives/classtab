Notes
-----

This is wrongly numbered Lesson 23 in my Thorlaksson edition.
The Flava Sala video is a teaching video and doesn't repeat the sections.
The tempo of the Flavio Sala video is 88bpm (quarter notes)
The tempo of the Norbert Neunzling video is 126 bpm (quarter notes).
The tempo of the MIDI is 120 bpm (quarter notes).
Lesson 22 is in 3 voices.
