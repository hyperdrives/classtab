Leo Delibes (1836-91) - Pizzicati from Sylvia

Tabbed by: The_S

The_S:     I stumbled across this and much enjoyed its simplicity.
           Along with the Midi, I found the sheet music, which is for
           two guitars, which didn't do me much good.
           So I have reduced it for one guitar.
           Unfortunately, the speed at which I wish to play this piece,
           makes it impossible to include and play the bass notes which
           accompany this basic tune. It's a shame because the bass notes
           make the song much more enjoyable to listen to.
           I play this song almost entirely with my thumb (right hand).
           After plucking each note, I immediately mute the string with
           my thumb, so as to create a pizzicato effect. The chords,
           however, I play normally.
           Have fun.

 Tuning: EADGBE (Standard)           Timing: 6/8 and as fast as you can :)
