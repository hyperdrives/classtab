 Ballet & Allegretto - Manual Ponce (1882-1948)

 Tabbed by: The_S

 The_S: I heard Segovia play this piece during a documentary called
        "The Song of the Guitar". This was when I first was interested
        in playing classical guitar, and I was more fascinated by the
        fast-paced songs and took little notice of pieces like this.
        Now I am a little more mature, and have found an elegance in this
        piece which I adore. Just the first few notes of the song catches
        my attention.
        Unfortunately, to allow for some bass notes to be sustained, there
        are some quick chord changes that will require a bit of practice to
        move to.
        The Midi sounds good enough, so you don't really need a recording
        to understand the timing and how the piece should sound. My advice
        is to try to keep the bass notes going for as long as you can


 Tuning: EADGBE (standard)

 Timing: 4/4
