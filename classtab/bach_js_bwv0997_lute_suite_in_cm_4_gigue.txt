J.S. Bach: Lute Suite No.2 (BWV 997) - Gigue

Corrections and re-formatting by: El Guapo - January 2002

This is my revision based on an earlier tab, which was in turn based on
Paul Vondiziano's interpretation of the song from http://mp3.com

For the most part, the original tab was pretty good, except here and there
a few measures were transposed too low, a few notes were missing, and the
formatting was sparse (and in the wrong time signature). This song can now
also be heard exactly on Weed's archive (classtab.org), as the new MIDI which
I am submitting with this is an exact playthrough of this tab.
