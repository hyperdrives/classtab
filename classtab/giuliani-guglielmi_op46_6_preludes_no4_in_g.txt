Notes and Legend
----------------
The left hand fingering below the tab is taken from the sheet. Much of the
other left hand positioning is my own, and could probably be improved.
The speed of the MIDI file is 80 bpm (quarter notes).

measure 4 - the 2nd pull-off isn't shown in the sheet
measure 46 - the 9th and 10th notes (G,D) are shown as a pull-off in the sheet
measures 47-50 - in the sheet the F#s have been hand corrected to F
measure 63 - in the sheet the bottom note of the chord is E (not G)

(There may be minor errors in the sheet which have not been picked up.)
