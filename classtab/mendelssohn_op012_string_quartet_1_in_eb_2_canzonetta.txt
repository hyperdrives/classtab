Tabbed by Yola Vanderwal and The_S - hermanss@iprimus.com.au - December 2002

The_S:  Anyone attempting to learn this piece is encouraged to obtain a
        recording. The timing of this tab is fairly precise, but a recording
        will help muchly. It is a most colourful and enjoying piece.

Yola:   This tab is based on the way Segovia transcribed and played the piece.
        When listening to his performance, I immediately fell in love with it.
        I also suggest everyone listen to Segovia's performance (or that of
        any of the great players); the subtle notes within the quick parts will
        probably touch your heart as well.
