NOTES

The accompanying MIDI is 120 bpm. It is usually played faster on the piano,
often about 135 bpm (Edwin Fischer), rarely as fast as 170 bpm (Glenn Gould).

The fingering follows the sheet music (found online), "Transcribed for guitar
by Richard Yates, 1999". There are some big stretches and many notes are easier
to play on the lower frets or with open strings, but this would change the
phrasing and affect hammer-ons and pull-offs. In some places the fingering
wasn't obvious, and there may have been mistakes in the sheet, so use the
fingering as a guide and amend it as necessary or as seems sensible.

"Jones" in the notes below refers to the piano sheet music published by the
Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music, "Edited and annotated by
Richard Jones, Commentaries by Donald Francis Tovey, 1994".
