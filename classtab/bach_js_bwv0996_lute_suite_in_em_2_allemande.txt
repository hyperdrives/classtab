Subject: J.S. Bach -- Allemande

Johann Sebastian Bach

Allemande from Lute Suite No 1 BWV 996

Submitted by Rod Kahler (browsers@flash.net)

This is one of my favorite songs to play on the guitar. It's in 4/4
timing, played fairly fast. Once you memorize the positions, it's pretty
easy to play. Add rest strokes when possible and attack those slurs.
You may want to listen to this song.  You can hear Segovia play it on
his "All Bach Performance", or you can download the midi file at
www.prs.net [now http://www.classicalarchives.com ]
