Johann Sebastian Bach
Bouree (double) from Partita No 1 for Solo Violin BWV 1002
Submitted by Rod Kahler

This double is the second version of the song Bouree (BWV 1002). It is
in common  time, and consists entirely of running 8th notes. C-roman
numeral means barre that fret. Listen to Segovia play it on his all Bach
performance CD, or download the midi file at the Classical Midi Archives
(www.prs.net).
