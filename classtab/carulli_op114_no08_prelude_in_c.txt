Legend

right hand fingering above tab      left hand fingering below tab
p - thumb                           1 - index
i - index                           2 - middle
m - middle                          3 - ring
a - ring                            4 - little

= - note held
s - slide right hand finger to the next note

CI.....  - full barre on the 1st fret
CI*....  - barre on the 1st fret leaving the inner strings open
CIII...  - full barre on the 3rd fret

mp       - mezzo-piano, half/medium soft
Rinf.    - rinforzando, volume suddenly increasing on one or a few notes/chords
mf       - mezzo-forte, half, medium loud
> > >    - decrescendo, getting softer
< < <    - crescendo, getting louder
