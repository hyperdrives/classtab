Scherzino Mexicano - Manuel Ponce

I searched the web for a long time to find an accurate tab of Scherzino
Mexicano and could never find one that sounded correct. I finally sat down
for a couple of hours and tabbed it out listening to the recording of John
Williams playing it.
It's not a very difficult piece to play once you sit down and study it for
a while because there are a lot of chordal patterns that repeat throughout
the whole song.

As for timing, well... this is my first tab, so you'll have to listen to the
song for it.  I'm quite sure most of it is correct. If you notice any typos
or mistakes please email them to me.

Enjoy. :-)
