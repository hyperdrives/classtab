Fingering mainly from the Berben edition.
There are no stacattos in the Berben and Thorlaksson editions.
The Thorlakkson edition repeats measures 12-15.

measure 2 - the sheet has the C on the 2nd beat as a quarter note
measure 4 - the sheet has the E on the 1st beat as a quarter note

Legend
------
left hand fingering below tab      right hand fingering above tab
1 - index finger                   p - thumb
2 - middle finger                  i - index finger
3 - ring finger                    m - middle finger
4 - little finger                  a - ring finger

====    - note held
.       - staccato - detached - the note is cut short
h       - hammer-on
p       - pull-off

f       - forte - loud
ff      - fortissimo - very loud
sf      - sforzando - forced, forcing, strongly accented

cI____  - part barre on the 1st fret with the 1st finger
cV____  - barre on the 5th fret with the 1st finger
cVIII_  - barre on the 9th fret with the 1st finger
