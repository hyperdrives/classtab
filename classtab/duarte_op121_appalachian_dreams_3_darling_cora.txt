Legend
------
measure 10 - sul pont - play close to the bridge
measure 18 - normale - return to playing normally (the end of the sul pont)
measure 30 - 5b - fret G#, push string sideways (bend) to reach A, then
             let it return slightly downwards, like a "wail"
measure 30 - 0* - pizzicato a la Bartok - pluck the string vertically by
             snapping it so that it rebounds off the fingerboard

===  - note held (duration)
/    - slide up
h    - hammer on
p    - pull off
cI   - part barre on the 1st fret
CI   - barre on the 1st fret
CII  - barre on the 2nd fret
cIII - part barre on the 3rd fret
CIII - barre on the 3rd fret
cV   - part barre on the 5th fret
CV   - barre on the 5th fret
