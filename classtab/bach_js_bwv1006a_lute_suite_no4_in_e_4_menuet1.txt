BWV 1006a, Suite #4, Minuet
MINUET: J.S. BACH
TRANSCRIBED BY: KEVIN MANGES (KevinM2556@aol.com)

I tried to include some fingering (under the TAB) to assist in keeping this
smooth.  There are quite a few pull-offs that I did not indicate, but a
listen to a recording and you'll find them easy to spot.  I have two versions
of this piece; the piece below and one much longer.  I chose to TAB the
shorter version because I'm lazy.  I've heard it recorded both ways.  It's a
pretty piece.  This looks best in Courrier font.
Enjoy.
