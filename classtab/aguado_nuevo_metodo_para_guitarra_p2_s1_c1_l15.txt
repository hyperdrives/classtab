Notes
-----
The tab is based on the Benito Campo 1843 edition.
Additional left hand fingering is taken from the Thorlaksson 2000 edition.

Aguado's wrote six 'Methods' - for information on three of them see -
"Dionisio Aguado - New Guitar Method - Introduction to the Tecla edition"
(Brian Jeffery, 1981)
http://www.tecla.com/extras/0001/0011/0011intro.htm

left hand fingering below the tab
1 - index
2 - middle
3 - ring
4 - little

E - 8th note
S - 16th note
T - 32nd note
. - dotted note (note held for its value plus half its value)
L - tied note (the note continues to be held for the additional time value)
