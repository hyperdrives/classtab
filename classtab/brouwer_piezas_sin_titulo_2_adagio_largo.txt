Legend
------

left hand fingering below tab
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===    held note
/      slide up
g      grace note(s)
h      hammer-on
p      pull-off
II__   barre on the 2nd fret

..3..  triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
- - >  (below the tab) decrescendo - getting softer
/.\    fermata - pause
-      tenuto
>      accent
,      breathe (pause)

<7>    natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 7th fret
<12>   natural harmonic - play open string, touch point on 12th fret

cantando   in a singing style
corto      short
delicato   delicately
eco        echo
mf         mezzo forte (medium loud)
mp         mezzo piano (medium soft)
p          piano (soft)
pp         pianissimo (very soft)
pesante    heavy, weighty, forcibly
