Arrangement: Christopher J. Rizzo

This is originally a piano piece by French composer Erik Satie.
It's played slowly and solemnly. This is also the third of three
movements in the piece. To hear this and the other two check out
Christopher Parkening's album "Parkening: Music of Two Centuries",
or to hear it on piano check out Roland Pontinen's "Erik Satie".
