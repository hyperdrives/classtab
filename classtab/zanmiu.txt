Dance of Miu

A Traditional Chinese Dance from the Clan of Miu

I found it in a classical guitar book which is published in China. It's a
very famous piece of music played on a Chinese music instrutment, the Pipa.
This is a simple version for guitar; the version played on the Pipa requires
advanced skills. Listen to the midi before you try to play it.
