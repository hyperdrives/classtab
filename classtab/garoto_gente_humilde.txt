Duration Legend
---------------
W      - whole note
H      - half note
Q      - quarter note
E      - 8th note
S      - 16th note
a      - acciaccatura
+      - tied note (the previous note is held for the extra value)
.      - dotted note
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 two notes

Duration letters will always appear directly above or below the notes (fret
numbers) they represents the duration for.  Duration letters with no fret
number above or below them represent rests.

Tablature Legend
----------------
 h   - hammer-on
 p   - pull-off
 s   - slide (from one note to another note)
 ^   - arpeggiate (ripple upwards from the lowest note to the highest note)
 /   - slide up
 \   - slide down
<12> - natural harmonic - open string, 12th fret
 ()  - tied note (the previous note is held for the extra value)
