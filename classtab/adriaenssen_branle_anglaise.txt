Notes and Legend
----------------
The branle is a French dance. This piece was written for the lute. It is often
played with a capo on the 3rd fret.
The tempo of the MIDI is 100 bpm (quarter notes).

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger
