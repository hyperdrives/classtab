Legend
------

left hand fingering below tab
1  - index

3  - ring
4  - little

=====    - note held
p        - pull-off
s        - slur (played as a slide down)
>        - accented, stressed
.        - staccato (detached, clipped - applies to all the notes beneath it)
:        - tenuto (held - note held for its full value, not staccato)

cI       - part barre on the 1st fret
CI       - barre on the 1st fret
cII      - part barre on the 2nd fret
cIII     - part barre on the 3rd fret
CIII     - barre on the 3rd fret
CIV      - barre on the 4th fret
cV       - part barre on the 5th fret
cVI      - part barre on the 6th fret
cVIII    - part barre on the 8th fret

<7>      - natural harmonic on the 7th fret
<12>     - natural harmonic on the 12th fret

< < <    - crescendo - becoming louder
> > >    - decrescendo - becoming softer
cresc.   - crescendo - becoming louder
dim.     - diminuendo - becoming gradually softer
f        - forte - loud
marcato  - stressed
mf       - mezzoforte - medium loud
mp       - mezzopiano - medium soft
p        - piano - soft
pp       - pianissimo - very soft
