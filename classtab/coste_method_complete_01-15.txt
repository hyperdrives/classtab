Here are the first 15 guitar studies from "Methode complete pour la Guitarre
par Ferdinand Sor" published with alterations by Napoleon Coste.

Try to understand each piece because they are technically easy, short and
you can play them with all your feeling of taste and imagination.

If you want to hear pieces from this collection, try to find them in the
repertoires of Segovia or J McFadden - I'm sure they play these pieces.

These pieces are all by Sor, but the last 6 of the 51 pieces in the "Complete
Guitar Method" are a suite written by Robert de Visee and arranged by N Coste.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
These pieces are published by The Guitar School (www.eythorsson.com)
