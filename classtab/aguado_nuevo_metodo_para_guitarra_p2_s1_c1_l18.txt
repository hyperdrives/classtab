Notes
-----

The tab is based on the Benito Campo 1843 edition with the following changes -

- additional left hand fingering taken from the Thorlaksson 2000 edition
- measures 1-7, 9-11, 13-15, 17-23: 1st, 3rd, 5th, 7th notes = quarter notes
- measure 21: first note (B) played on the 3rd string (as elsewhere)

Aguado's wrote six 'Methods' - for information on three of them see -
"Dionisio Aguado - New Guitar Method"
Introduction to the Tecla edition (Brian Jeffery, 1981)
http://www.tecla.com/extras/0001/0011/0011intro.htm

The tempo of the MIDI and the Norbert Neunzling video is 80 bpm (quarter notes).
