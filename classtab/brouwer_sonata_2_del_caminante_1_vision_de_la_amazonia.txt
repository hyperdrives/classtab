Legend
------
=      - duration of note (length of time)
h      - hammer-on
p      - pull-off
s      - slide
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes in the time of 2 notes
|-5-|  - pentuplet - 5 notes in the time of 4 notes
<7>    - natural harmonic on the open string, right hand points to the 7th fret
<12>   - natural harmonic on the open string, right hand points to the 12th fret
