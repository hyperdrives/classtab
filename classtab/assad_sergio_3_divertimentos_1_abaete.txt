Notes and Legend
----------------
Sheet edition: Guitar Solo Publications GSP-244 (San Francisco, 2005)

left hand fingering (below the tab) -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===    - note held (duration)
*      - tapping left hand with slight distortion
//     - slide up
\\     - slide down
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
>      - (above the tab) accented, stressed
arp.   - arpeggiate - ripple upwards from the lowest note (using index finger)
g      - grace note (played just before the main note and immediately released)
h      - hammer-on
p      - pull-off
<7>    - natural harmonic on the 7th fret

< < <  - crescendo - growing louder
> > >  - decrescendo - growing softer
cresc. - crescendo - growing louder
mf     - mezzo forte - moderately loud

CIII   - barre on the 3rd fret with the 1st finger
CVI    - barre on the 6th fret with the 1st finger
