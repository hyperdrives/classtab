Notes and Legend
----------------
There is no key signature; the piece is close to G major.
The tempo of the N Neunzling video and the MIDI file is 184 bpm (quarter notes).

===           - note held (duration)
< < <         - crescendo (growing louder)
> > >         - decrescendo (growing softer)
f             - forte - loud
mf            - mezzo-forte - moderately loud
mp            - mezzo-piano - moderately soft
rall.         - rallentando - gradual slowing down
cresc. molte  - becoming much louder
