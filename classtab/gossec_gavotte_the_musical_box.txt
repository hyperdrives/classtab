Notes + Legend
--------------
Order of play: 1-8, 1-8, 9-16, 17-24, 17-24, 25-32, 1-16
The left hand fingering isn't given in the sheet. There are many possibilities.
Playing all the pull-offs and hammer-ons may not be reasonable, so slides can
be substituted (or the notes can be picked).
Most of the notes are played staccato, except where indicated. The tab has many
notes on open strings which makes the piece easier to play, but to get a crisp
staccato effect it is usually better to use closed strings.
The tempo of the MIDI file is 138 bpm (quarter notes).

The sheet has the last note of measure 32 as F#, which sounds a bit weak to my
ears. If you want something more definite to end the section, you could change
it to D (open 5th string), with or without an octave D on the 3rd string.
