Prelude in G Major - George Yeatman (1914-2004)

"George Yeatman was born March 2, 1914 and lives in Battle Creek, Michigan.
He wrote Prelude in G while he was partners with Aaron Shearer. Aaron Shearer
asked him to write a simple beginner piece." - Matt Cosgrove

Recordings of other George Yeatman compositions at -
http://www.cosgrovemusic.com/sound_recordings.htm

original tab by Rene Puchinger in 1998 - "This one is a little affected by
jazz music. I like to play it not slow but not fast - as a river flow..."
rene.puchinger@seznam.cz - http://citadel.wz.cz
