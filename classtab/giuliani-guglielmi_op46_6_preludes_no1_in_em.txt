Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the Jon Mendle video is ~104 bpm (quarter notes).
The tempo of the Emily Shaw video is ~84 bpm (quarter notes).
The tempo of the MIDI is 88 bpm (quarter notes).
The Boije 556 edition has hand-corrections for measures 8, 21, 22 and 29.

left hand fingering below the tab
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===    - note held, length of note
f      - (below the tab) forte - loud
p      - (below the tab) piano - soft
sf     - sforzando - strongly accented, suddenly loud
> > >  - crescendo - growing louder
|-6-|  - sextuplet - six notes played in the time of 4 notes
CI___  - barre on the 1st fret
CII__  - barre on the 2nd fret
CIII_  - barre on the 3rd fret
CIV__  - barre on the 4th fret
CV___  - barre on the 5th fret
