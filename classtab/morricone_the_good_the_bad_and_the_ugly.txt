The Good, The Bad & The Ugly - Ennio Morricone (1928-)

This version of The Good, The Bad and The Ugly is based on the California
Guitar Trio version from the album Invitation which is awesome.  I did the
arrangement for two guitars.  The only tab I could get my hands on was in
a Guitar Magazine that had famous guitar movie riffs and it had the main
theme (the spaghetti western guitar part).  So I wound up picking most of
it out by ear which was trying because on the CGT version they tune up a
half step which is very weird.  That being said, I know that I probably
didn't get the piece down note perfect, especially the interlude which is
very hard to figure out.  So if you can figure out any places where I
totally botched up the piece let me know.

Fourfox@earthlink.net
