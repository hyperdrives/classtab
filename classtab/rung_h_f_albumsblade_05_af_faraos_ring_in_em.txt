Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the Norbert Neunzling vid and the MIDI is 56 bpm (quarter notes).

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===   - note held, length of note
< <   - crescendo - growing louder
> >   - decrescendo - growing softer
arp.  - rippling upwards from the lowest note to the highest note
dim.  - diminuendo - diminishing, gradually getting softer
p     - (below the tab) piano - soft
pp    - (below the tab) pianissimo - very soft
rall. - rallentando - slowing down, getting slower and becoming gradually softer
