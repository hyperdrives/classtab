*************************************************************
Pictures at an Exhibition - Promenade I, by Modest Mussorgsky
*************************************************************

Here is the beginning of this crazy arrangement by Kazuhito Yamashita,
with the original fingerings. This is very, very difficult to play and as
far as I know there isn't any perfect interpretation of this arrangement
yet recorded on guitar but there are some great ones such as this linked
video by Jorge Caballero. There aren't many performance tips included here,
mostly because the original text is mostly written in Mandarin!

Transcribed from the sheet music by Maxime Bergeron (mazout360@hotmail.com)

Video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wa8Ghy1Pz2k - Jorge Caballero

tuning: DADGBE   time: 5/4 & 6/4   tempo: Allegro giusto, nel modo russico...
