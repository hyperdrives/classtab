Title: Largo from "Winter"
Composer: Antonio Vivaldi
Tabbed by ShadowJim
Email: shadowsinterlude at gmail

Everyone knows this, or they should.  The piece is easy enough,
but I've added some duplicated notes in the basline, to
give the piece a fuller sound (to be fair, it was my guitar
teachers idea).

Notes:
- In measure 5, the E in the A major chord I added myself.
- In measure 8, the sustained trill with bassnote over it is
  hard to put into a tab properly.  The idea is to sustain the
  trill the duration of a dotted quarter note.  (Listen to the midi.)
- Trills in baroque music start on the note above, so e.g.
  in measure 8 start on the C# (2nd fret) and end on B (open).
