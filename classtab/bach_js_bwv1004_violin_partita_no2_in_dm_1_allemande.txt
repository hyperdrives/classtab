Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
There are many different arrangements of this piece, some with added bass notes,
and each with their own style of fingering. I have kept to the Mosoczi Miklos
arrangement (but haven't included hammer-ons and pull-offs). It is easier to
play by making more use of open strings, but the phrasing may suffer. If you
want inpiration for which ornaments to add, listen to the David Russel audio!

The repeats are not present in the MIDI.
The tempo of the MIDI is 50 bpm (quarter notes).

left hand fingering below the tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger
