Fellow Guitarists,

Here's my first TAB draft for Brahms' "Hungarian Dance No.5 in F# Minor".
This is a standard you'll quickly recognize from the opening theme. Please
let me know if this works for you and of any editing needed. Also, if you
have any other classical, traditional, folk or other favorites you'd like
to see drop me a note. I'm always looking for arrangement projects.

Check in at American Primitive Guitar http://home.sprynet.com/sprynet/leswaia
for a listing of other tunes I've arranged, including some originals.  Also,
contact me about the tape swap if you've got some recordings of your own work
to share.

I usually keep up to date on the newsgroup rec.music.makers.guitar.tablature
if you want to make a public comment on my project there.  Use APG in subject
line to catch fellow afficianadoes eye.

Les at APG
