Duration Legend
---------------
H - half; Q - quarter; E - 8th; S - 16th; T - 32nd; X - 64th; a - acciaccatura
+ - note tied to previous; . - note dotted; .. - note double dotted
Irregular groupings are notated above the duration line
Duration letters appear directly above the notes/fret numbers they represent.
Duration letters with no fret number below them represent rests
Low melody durations appear below the staff

Tablature Legend
----------------
 h   - hammer-on
 p   - pull-off
 =   - hold note
 x   - muted

Misc Legend
-----------
 |  - bar
||  - double bar
||o - repeat start
o|| - repeat end
*|  - double bar (ending)
 :  - bar (freetime)
 $  - Segno
 &  - Coda
