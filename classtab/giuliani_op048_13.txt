Legend
------

left hand fingering below the tab        right hand fingering above the tab
1 - index finger                         p - thumb
2 - middle finger                        i - index finger
3 - ring finger                          m - middle finger
4 - little finger                        a - annular (ring) finger

=====   - note held
acc.    - acciaccatura - a 'grace note' -- the note is played immediately before
          or at the same time as the following note
p       - pull-off

CI____  - full barre on the 1st fret
cII___  - part barre on the 2nd fret
CIII__  - full barre on the 3rd fret
CIV___  - full barre on the 4th fret
CVII__  - full barre on the 7th fret
CVIII_  - full barre on the 8th fret

< < <   - crescendo - becoming louder
> > >   - decrescendo - becoming softer
