key
---

Allegro moderato - lively, but not too fast

==== - note is held

CII  - barre on 2nd fret with 1st finger
CVII - barre on 7th fret with 1st finger

fingering below the tab:   1 - 1st (fore) finger
                           2 - 2nd (index) finger
                           3 - 3rd (ring) finger
                           4 - 4th (little) finger (pinkie)

expression below the tab: pp - pianissimo - very quiet
                           p - piano - soft
                          mf - mezzo-forte - medium
                           f - forte - loud
                       cresc - crescendo - growing louder
                     decresc - decrescendo - growing softer
                          sf - sforzando - emphasised
                          rf - rinforzando - suddenly louder
                    poco rit - poco ritardando - becoming slower

notes
-----

measure 7 - barre top 3 strings on 2nd fret with 1st finger
