Notes
-----

Editions used - Berben 1354, Ancona, Italy
                Boije 138 - Haslinger 2113, Vienna
                Deutscher Verlag fur Musik - DVfM 32068
                Hebe a11038 (Jeffery) - Tecla 0105, London (2002)
                Kruger (1906)
                Thorlaksson 1080 (2001)

The time signature is 4/4 in the Berben, Kruger and Thorlaksson editions.
The phrasing and the left hand fingering varies in the different editions.
Suggested tempo - at least 56 bpm (half notes), if you can manage it :)

measure 3 - the 1st note is E (6th string,open) in the Berben edition
measure 16 - in the sheet the 1st, 5th and 9th notes are 1/4 notes
measure 18 - the 1st note is a 1/4 note in the Boije, Kruger & Hebe editions
measures 18,19,20 - these are repeated in the Thorlaksson edition

Legend

left hand fingering below the tab

p      - pull-off
