Legend
------

H - half note

NH    - natural harmonic
<12>  - (octave higher), open string, 12th fret

AH    - artificial harmonic
5<17> - (octave higher), left hand 5th fret, right hand points to the 17th fret
7<19> - (octave higher), left hand 7th fret, right hand points to the 19th fret
