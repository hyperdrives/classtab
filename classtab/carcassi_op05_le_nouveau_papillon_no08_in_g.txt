Subject: TAB: Andantino Grazioso by Carcassi (1792-1853)

This is an Andantino Grazioso by Matteo Carcassi.  It is a bouncy but
graceful piece (grazioso is Italian for graceful).  It is in 3/8 time,
'|'s mark the 8th note beats and the '.'s mark the 16th note beats.
32nd notes a placed proportionally between them.

I'm pretty sure this is accurate, let me know if you find a mistake.

Op 5, Le Nouveau Papillon - No 8, Andantino Grazioso in G       Matteo Carcassi
