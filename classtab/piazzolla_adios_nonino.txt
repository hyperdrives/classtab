ADIOS NONINO - Tango - Astor Piazzolla (1921 - 1992)

Arranged by Hugo Romero

Tablature by Hernán G. Renom - dasein79@hotmail.com - 11 September 2002

This one is an easy (but still beautiful) version that can be played by
intermediate guitarists. If you want to hear a wonderful version (which
is very difficult to play), I recommend listening to the recording by the
great Argentinian guitarist, Cacho Tirao.

There is an excellent biography of Astor Piazzolla and an online recording
(real audio file) of Astor Piazzolla and his Quintet playing Adios Nonino at
http://www.piazzolla.org/biography/biography-english.html

Tuning: Standard
Key: E Minor
Time Signature: 3/4
S: Segno
