Legend

right hand fingering above tab      left hand fingering below tab
p - thumb                           1 - index
i - index                           2 - middle
m - middle                          3 - ring
a - ring                            4 - little

/ - slide up
\ - slide down

(--3--) - triplet

rit. - ritardando (slowing down gradually)
D. C. al fine - repeat from the beginning to 'fine'
