This is a duet for nylon guitars in standard tuning.  Piazzolla's Tango Suite
was written for Sergio and Odair Assad. This piece (Andante) is the second of
three movements. It is the easiest to play, but also perhaps the most beautiful
and moving from the suite. Play with pure, clean tone and much feeling.

please send any corrections or comments to wimiam at yahoo dot com  :-)

Duration Legend
---------------

W - whole    H - half    Q - quarter    E - 8th    S - 16th    T - 32nd
a - acciaccatura   + - note tied to previous   . - note dotted

Irregular groupings are notated above the duration line. Duration letters will
always appear directly above the note/fret numbers they represent the duration
for.  Duration letters with no fret number below them represent rests. Low
melody durations appear below the staff

Tablature Legend
----------------

 h   - hammer-on
 p   - pull-off
 /\  - slide into or out of (from/to "nowhere")
 s   - legato slide
<n>  - natural harmonic
[n]  - artificial harmonic
 =   - hold bend; also acts as connecting device for hammers/pulls
