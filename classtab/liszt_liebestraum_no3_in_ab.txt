Liebestraum by Franz Liszt (1811 - 1886)
~~~~~~~~~~~
Arranged by David Atkinson November 1994.

Retune the low E string to D to play this piece.
The 'm's denote the melody since as the arpeggios often
overlap them they can be easily overlooked.
The melody note (in this case the highest note beneath
the 'm') should be emphasized and extracted from the
accompaniment.

p = pull-off
h = hammer-on
s = slide
=== = sustain

Time signature is 6/4.

Please email mistakes, suggestions and fan mail ;) to:
kaum0@central.susx.ac.uk

Enjoy...
