wonderful piece arranged for classical guitar by Göran Söllscher -
very dreamy and relaxed.

tabbed for weed's Classical Guitar Tablature page 
'cause i couldn't believe it wasn't there!
thanks to john kean (his was the original tab in my 
head, had memorised years ago)

Drop-D tuning [DADGBE low to high]
4/4 time

above the tab are the | and . which represent beat counts...
play with a slight jazzy sort of swing in the beat and 
don't forget to drop that D

a ^ under a chord in the tab means to 'arpeggiate' the chord.  
an example for those not familiar with arpeggios...
