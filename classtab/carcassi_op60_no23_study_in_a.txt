Notes
-----
Sheet editions used - Boije - Mayence B Schott's Sohne, 11423
                      Edson Lopes (2011)
                      Thorlaksson, 1007

Left and right fingering is taken from the Lopes edition.

"More virtuoso scales, perhaps not quite so hard this time, and stabilized by a
 bass which takes on more interest in the middle section (bar 17 on)."
Tecla edition notes, 2006 - http://www.tecla.com/extras/0001/0345/notes.htm

Order of play - measures 1-20 followed by measures 1-8.

Legend
------
left hand fingering below the tab        right hand fingering above the tab
1 - index finger                         p - thumb
2 - middle finger                        i - index finger
3 - ring finger                          m - middle finger
4 - little finger                        a - annular (ring) finger

======  note duration (how long the note is held)
h       hammer-on
p       pull-off

CVIII_  barre on the 8th fret (played with the 1st finger)

< < <   becoming louder
> > >   becoming softer
cresc.  crescendo - becoming louder
D.C.    Da Capo - go back to the beginning
Fine    finish (on repeat)
mf      mezzo forte - medium loud
p       piano - soft
