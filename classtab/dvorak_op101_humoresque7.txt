Humoresque (Op 101, No 7)
By Antonin Dvorak (1841-1904)
Tabbed by Richard Finlan - Feb 2003.
This well known tune is not too difficult but
it might take some practice to get the lento/legato correct.
It's in 4/4 time, in the key of G.
Note: The duff notes that crop up are deliberate as
this piece is intended to be humourous.
I hope you enjoy it, but don't put any naughty words to it.
