Title: Ausencias
Artist: Astor Piazzolla
Arranged: Aner Lucero - http://anerman.blogspot.com

Hi every one, this is a very nice Piazzolla's pice that I thought could work on
the guitar. The original piece (or at least the recording I've found) is played
by bandoneon and bass-guitar. In this arrangement the melody follows closely
the original one and the bass tries to mimic the one on the recording I've got,
but it is not very precise.

This tablature covers only the first part of three.
