Sylvius Leopold Weiss - Fantaisie en Re mineur / Fantasia in D minor SW 82.2
Adagio, Presto, Adagio

An early medium/easy piece from the most famous German baroque Lutenist,
arranged for the guitar by myself. The first two parts (Adagio and Presto)
are played 4/4, and the last Adagio is played 3/4. Note duration indication
is Guitar Pro style - see the legend below the score.
A midi file is also available.

Guitar tuning - D A D G B E (re la re sol si mi)

contact me if you want the .gp5 file version for this piece.
