In addition to Stinky10's tab, this is my rendition of Lullabye, and my first
tab.  It uses the basic chords below.  C3 is just C barred on the third fret.
The chords are indicated above the tab for easy placing of the fingers.  In
some parts, you only have to lift or add a finger to the chord.

You can add style to your rendition by running down the chords slowly when it
is tabbed completely.   The extra notes, like the last two notes in the
example, are added just for adlib.

Please feel free to email me for corrections.

ENJOY!
