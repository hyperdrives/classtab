Duration Legend
---------------
H     - half note
Q     - quarter note
E     - 8th note

Tablature Legend
----------------
g    - grace note
h    - hammer on
L    - tied note
p    - pull off
s    - slide
/    -  slide into from below or out of upwards
\    -  slide into from above or out of downwards
NH   - natural harmonic
<7>  - natural harmonic on the open string, right hand points to the 7th fret
<12> - octave harmonic on the open string, right hand points to the 12th fret
