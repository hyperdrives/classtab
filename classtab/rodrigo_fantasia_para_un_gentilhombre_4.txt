FANTASIA PARA UN GENTILHOMBRE - by Joaquin Rodrigo

Tab by John Mason - October 2003

Having fully transcribed Rodgigo's 'Concierto de Aranjuez' last year,I
had many messages from people asking if I could transcribe this great piece.
Here it is! It's very long and it's very difficult!
Its impossible to indicate timing and phrasing with TAB, but the length
of the bars will hopefully help you navigate around this transcription.
Also, barre chords are indicated where the left-hand fingering isn't
immediately obvious.
Feedback and questions are most welcome at johnmason007@hotmail.com
Happy Playing!!! - John M

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
NOTES -
* The bars are numbered as in the musical score; shown beneath the first
  bar of each stave.
* The length of orchestral breaks are indicated as "orch # bars".
* "4tr6" is a rapid trill hammering (in this example) from fret 4 to 6.
* Slide up = /
* Slide down = \
* All standard tuning EXCEPT for the last movement Canarios (bottom E to D)
* "F5" = full barre at fret 5 (shown above the bar)
* "H5" = half barre at fret 5 (shown above the bar)
* "n.h" = natural harmonic
* "a.h" = artificial harmonic
* Hammer-on = h; Pull-off = p
