Time is 4/4 unless posted explicitly otherwise

Tuning DADGBD

# = harmonic    ^ = hammer on    v = pull off    === = sustain    / = slide
19# = left hand fret 7, right hand fret 19

This is the original Sunburst Intro. The end rolls right into the begining
of the  acclaimed "Sunburst" which you can find all over.  This is fun to play,
very lyrical, inspiring and fresh. Watch those 'signature' York RH harmonics
sprinkled in, and endevor to play it evenly in the flow. Let those open notes
ring where possible.

Permit me to add that I wouldn't be the truly amazing well rounded guitarist
(chuckle) that I am today without our great little tab community out there to
pinch from now and again. That said it's only fair to give one back once and
awhile. Ok, lets play....
