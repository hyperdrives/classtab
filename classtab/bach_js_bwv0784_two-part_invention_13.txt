BWV 784 Invention No.13 in Am (2-part) - JS Bach

This song is extremely difficult to play.  It's really meant to be played as
a duet. Try to hold the base notes as long as possible (it helps to make it
sound like two different instruments.) Listen to the midi file at www.prs.net
to get an idea of how it sounds. Play it as fast as possible.

Transcribed by Rod Kahler....gkahler@flash.net           good luck
