************************************************************************
Tonadilla Op.170 No.5 (To Andres Segovia), by Mario Castelnuovo-Tedesco
************************************************************************

The "themes" for this tonadilla are built using two chromatic scales from
low A (5th string) to high C (1st string 8th fret) and from high A (1st
string 5th fret) down to low F# (6th string 2nd fret), where each note
has a letter attributed to it (A to Z, including CH, LL and N-tilde) in
each scale. Castelnuovo then built 4 themes using the ANDRES and SEGOVIA
note progression from each of these scales.

Duration Legend
---------------
H      - half note
Q      - quarter note
E      - 8th note
S      - 16th note
|-3-|  - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes

Tablature Legend
----------------
h      - hammer on
L      - tied note
p      - pull off
NH     - natural harmonic
<7>    - natural harmonic on the 12th fret
<12>   - natural harmonic on the 12th fret
AH     - artificial harmonic
5<17>  - artificial harmonic, left hand 5th fret, right hand points to 17th fret
6<18>  - artificial harmonic, left hand 6th fret, right hand points to 18th fret
