Notes and Legend
----------------

The lute tuning was probably in G, equivalent to using a capo on the 3rd fret.
Zamboni's pieces are sometimes played with the 3 bottom strings tuned an octave
lower. The guitar sheet fingering tries to reflect the original lute music.
Some of the stretches and held notes show what the composer intended, but may
not be the easiest way to play them on a guitar.

The sheet does not give a tempo. The suggested tempo of 84 bpm is based on the
YouTube performance by Divino Kaps.

Suggested order of play: 0-16, 0-35, 31-36, 16-35, 31-36

Legend
------

p      - pull-off
8      - (below the tab) additonal bass note one octave lower in the lute sheet
