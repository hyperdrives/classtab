notes
-----

Edition used - Simrock 2288 (Boije 487)
The Stich & Druck (9103) and Messonnier editions are the same as the Simrock.

measures 4, 8, 16 - in the sheet the middle note of the 1st chord is shown as
as a quarter note, but there is no easy fingering

The Castro edition (Journal de Musique Étrangère) has the following variations -

- measures 1,2,4-6,9,10,13,14 have a pull-off from the 4th to the 5th notes
- measure 4 has no staccatos
- the 5th note of measure 7 has a top F
- measure 8 is the same as measure 16
- the 7th note of measure 13 goes down to E (not back up to G)
- measure 15 is different -
