Notes & Legend
--------------
The lute tuning was probably in G, equivalent to using a capo on the 3rd fret.
The guitar sheet fingering tries to reflect the original lute music.
The tempo of the Divino Kaps video is 54 bpm (quarter notes).
The tempo of the MIDI is 66 bpm (quarter notes).

===  - note held
##   - an ornament on the upper note is indicated in the original
h    - hammer-on
p    - pull-off
8    - (below the tab) additonal bass note one octave lower in the lute sheet
