Notes and Legend
----------------
The tempo of the Norbert Neunzling vid and the MIDI is 54 bpm (quarter notes).

left hand fingering below tab -
1 - index finger
2 - middle finger
3 - ring finger
4 - little finger

===   - length of note
/     - slide
'     - (above the tab) staccato - detached, notes not held for their full value
|-3-| - triplet - 3 notes played in the time of 2 notes
g     - grace note - ornament - extra note(s) played at the same time as,
        or just before the main note, played as hammer-on or pull-off
CII_  - barre on the 2nd fret with the 1st finger
CIV_  - barre on the 4th fret with the 1st finger
