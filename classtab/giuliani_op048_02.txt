Here is another study by Mauro Giuliani, the 'divine guitarist', to be played
on the high frets - it will be funny once mastered. The notes are all 1/16
except for the last which is 1/2.
The RHF isn't problematic because it is always the same: p i m i. Enjoy!

Signature: La minore (A minor)
Tuning: Mi Si Sol Re La Mi (EBGDAE)
Time: 4/4

MAURO GIULIANI (BISCEGLIE 1781 - NAPLES 1829)

Moderato
