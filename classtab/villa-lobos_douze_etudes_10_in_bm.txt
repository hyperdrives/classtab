Etude No 10 - Heitor Villa-Lobos (1887-1959)

as recorded by Narciso Yepes

based on a Power Tab transcription by LNHaONCDN
(in memory of his friend and teacher Goran Krkic)

****************************************************************************
"The original manuscripts were changed a fair bit when edited by Segovia
for the Max Eschig editions, which are the ones that most tabs and various
sheets are derived from. The main things left out were the tempo and dynamic
directions... and also etude 10 has an entire passage omitted."
- John McDonnell (18 May 2009)
****************************************************************************

note the many changes of time signature at the start and end
