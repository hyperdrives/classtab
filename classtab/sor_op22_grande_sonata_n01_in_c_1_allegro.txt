transcribed by tarek yazbek

piece, in the key of C, in standard tuning. Mostly it's easy to play but there
are some tricky parts and hard chords to hold, but they will come along with
practice. Anyway enjoy, and for questions, corrections and suggestions
email me at yabek999@hotmail.com
