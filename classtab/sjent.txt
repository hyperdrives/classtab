parts as played using the left and right hands on the piano. Guitar 1 will be
played as the right hand side while guitar 2 will be played as the left hand
side. As for the timing, try to catch it yourself and if u face any problems,
feel free to contact me and I'll try my best to help.. Bye !

Enjoy !
