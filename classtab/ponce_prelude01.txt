notes
-----

two versions of the sheet music were used, French (Fr) and Russian (Ru)
hammer-ons and pull-offs have been used for slurs up and slurs down

2nd measure, 1st beat - Ru has A,F#
17th measure - Fr doesn't have 'a tempo' (return to original time)
20th measure, 3rd beat - Ru has B open 2nd string
23rd measure - Ru has 'rall' (slowing down), not 'senza rall'

legend
------

right hand fingering above tab
p - thumb
i - index
m - middle
a - ring

left hand fingering below tab
1 - index
2 - middle
3 - ring
4 - little

h = hammer on
p = pull off
/ = slide up to next note
\ = slide down from previous note
> = notes underneath are stressed

CI   - barre on the first fret
CII  - barre on the 2nd fret
CV   - barre on the 5th fret
CVII - barre on the 7th fret

http://www.classtab.org
