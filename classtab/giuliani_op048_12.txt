Notes
-----

Editions used - Berben 1354, Ancona, Italy
                Boije 138 - Haslinger 2113 , Vienna
                RiBS  - Emil Kruger, Roder, Leipzig, 1906
                Thorlaksson, Guitar School Iceland 1079, 2001

(The different editions do not agree on left hand fingering.)

It is marked Allegretto, but when it is played it appears to be faster. The
MIDI speed (76 bpm) is slower than the suggested speed (116 bpm) so that it
is easier to hear the individual notes.

The emphasis in sextupplets can be on just the 1st note, or on the 1st & 4th
notes, or on the 1st, 3rd & 5th notes. The emphasis in this piece is best
placed on the 1st & 4th notes as the 4th note carries the melody, but in
some performances where the piece is played very fast it sounds as though
the emphasis is only on the first note of the sextuplet.
