Legend and Notes
----------------
The tempo of the Zoran Anic vid and the MIDI is 146 bpm (dotted quarter notes).

right hand fingering above tab      left hand fingering below tab
p - thumb                           1 - index
i - index                           2 - middle
m - middle                          3 - ring
a - ring                            4 - little

===    - note held
/      - slide up
h      - hammer-on
p      - pull-off
<12>   - open (octave) harmonic on the 12th fret
cI     - part barre on the 1st fret with the 1st finger
cII    - part barre on the 2nd fret with the 1st finger
CII    - full barre on the 2nd fret with the 1st finger
cIII   - part barre on the 3rd fret with the 1st finger
cIV    - part barre on the 4th fret with the 1st finger
cV     - part barre on the 5th fret with the 1st finger
cVII    - part barre on the 7th fret with the 1st finger
cVIII   - part barre on the 8th fret with the 1st finger
< < <  - crescendo (growing louder)
> > >  - decrescendo (growing softer)
f      - forte (loud)
mf     - mezzo forte (moderately loud)
mp     - mezzo piano (moderately soft)
p      - (below the tab) piano (soft)
sf     - sforzando (strongly accented)
