Notes and Legend
----------------
Tabbed from the Simrock 2815 edition.
The grace notes in measures 23,26 look like appoggiaturas (passing notes) in the
Simrock 2815 edition but are shown as acciaccaturas in other editions. 
The tempo of the Andrey Bugrov vid & the MIDI is 84 bpm (dotted quarter notes).

left hand fingering (below tab)   1 - index
                                  2 - middle
                                  3 - ring
                                  4 - little

===  - note held
g    - grace note - acciaccatura, played as a pull-off just before the
       following note and then immediately released
p    - pull-off
