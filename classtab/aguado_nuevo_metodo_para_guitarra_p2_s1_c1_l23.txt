Notes
-----
This is wrongly numbered Lesson 24 in my Thorlaksson edition.
The Flava Sala video is half speed and doesn't repeat the sections.
Lesson 23 is in 3 voices.

In the Thorlakssohn edition the open Es on the 1st string are played on the
5th fret of the 2nd string, except in measures 11 and 15.
