Notes and Legend
----------------
The Boije edition was used for measures 7, 11 and 13.
The tempo of the the MIDI is 120 bpm (eighth notes).
Neither edition of the sheet music indicates the left hand fingering, and there
are various ways of playing it - you can probably improve on the tab fingering.

For measures 8 (first play) and 24 and 32 (repeats), both sheet editions only
have 2 beats, instead of the 3 beats indicated by the time signature. The MIDI
uses 1/8th note rests to maintain the Waltz rhythm. [comments welcome]
