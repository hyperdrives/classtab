Notes and Legend
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
There are errors in the sheet left hand fingering. I did my best to correct
obvious mistakes but some I'm not sure about, and there may be others I missed.
The tempo of the MIDI and the Yoram Zerbib audio is 100 bpm (quarter notes).

measure 17 - the sheet has F (not F#) for the 14th and 16th notes

measure 22 - this follows the sheet, and is how Yoram Zerbib plays it -
             but sharpening the F and the D sounds better to my ear -

< <    - (below the tab) crescendo - growing louder

f      - (below the tab) forte - loud
