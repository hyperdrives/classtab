Legend
------

left hand fingering below tab
1  - index
2  - middle
3  - ring
4  - little

=====   - note held
cI      - part barre on the 1st fret
cII     - part barre on the 2nd fret
h       - hammer-on
F       - fermata - pause (note/notes held up to twice their value)

 _5_
|||||   - 5 notes played in the time of 4 notes (quintuplet/quintolet/pentuplet)

a tempo - back to the original speed (after slowing down)
mf      - mezzo-forte - half/medium loud
mp      - mezzo piano - half/medium soft
p       - piano - soft
rit.    - ritenuto - slowing down (slowing down suddenly)
< < <   - crescendo - becoming louder
> > >   - decrescendo - becoming softer
